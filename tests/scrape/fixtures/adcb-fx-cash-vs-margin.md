# ADCB TouchPoints Credit Card

Earn ADCB TouchPoints on every dirham you spend.

## Key features

- 4 TouchPoints per AED 1 spent on dining
- 2 TouchPoints per AED 1 spent on groceries
- Complimentary airport lounge access twice per quarter

## Welcome offer

Earn 10,000 ADCB TouchPoints when you spend AED 5,000 in your first 60 days.

## Fees and charges

Annual fee: AED 525.

Cash deposit/withdrawal in foreign currency: 0.525% flat.

Foreign Currency transaction margin: 2.99% on every non-AED purchase.

VAT on the foreign currency transaction fee: 5% applied to the fee
component.

Interest rate: 3.25% per month on outstanding balances.

## Eligibility

Minimum monthly salary AED 8,000 for UAE residents.

No salary transfer required.
