# FAB World Elite Mastercard

Earn unlimited rewards on every dirham you spend with the FAB World Elite Mastercard.

## Key features

- Complimentary access to 1,000+ airport lounges worldwide
- 24/7 dedicated concierge service
- Buy 1 Get 1 cinema tickets at VOX Cinemas
- Travel insurance on every booking

## Welcome offer

Welcome bonus: Earn 40,000 FAB Rewards points on AED 10,000 spend within 60 days of card activation.

## Fees and charges

Annual fee: First year complimentary, AED 2,500 thereafter.

The annual fee for the FAB World Elite Mastercard is AED 0 in the first year. From year 2 onwards, the standard annual fee of AED 2,500 applies.

FX fee: 1.99% on every non-AED transaction.

Interest rate: 3.25% per month on outstanding balances.

## Eligibility

Minimum salary AED 30,000 per month for UAE residents.

No salary transfer required.

Minimum age: 21.
