# ADCB Schedule of Charges — Consolidated Credit Cards

This document lists the standard fees applicable to all ADCB credit cards.

## Annual Membership Fee

| Card | Annual Membership Fee |
|---|---|
| ADCB SimplyCash Credit Card | AED 314 |
| ADCB TouchPoints Credit Card | AED 525 |
| ADCB Etihad Guest Above Credit Card | AED 1,575 |
| ADCB Etihad Guest Beyond Credit Card | AED 2,625 |
| ADCB Privilege Club Visa Infinite | AED 1,575 |
| ADCB LuLu Platinum Credit Card | AED 314 |

The Annual Membership Fee is charged on issuance and on each card anniversary thereafter. Annual Membership Fee may be waived subject to spend thresholds — refer to the individual card terms.

## Foreign Currency Transaction Fee

A fee of 2.49% applies on all foreign currency transactions, plus 5% VAT on the fee component (effectively 0.1245% VAT-on-FX line item).

## Late Payment Fee

AED 241 (inclusive of VAT) on any payment received after the due date.

## Cash Advance Fee

3.15% of the cash advance amount or AED 99, whichever is higher.
