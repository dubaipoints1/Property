Emirates Skywards has opened a summer-long promotion, **"Season of Rewards,"** running **21 May to 31 August 2026** — a bundle of four member offers spanning earning and redemption. For UAE residents already inside the Skywards ecosystem, one of the four is broadly useful and the rest are situational.

## The one that matters for most members: Cash+Miles doubles

Through 31 August, 2,000 Skywards Miles unlock **USD 30** of value (about **AED 110**) on Emirates and flydubai bookings, instead of the usual USD 15 (about AED 55). It applies to fares and to extras — excess baggage, lounge access and seat selection — across all cabins and fare brands on both airlines. If you hold a modest Miles balance and fly Emirates or flydubai over the summer, this is the offer to actually use.

## Tier miles get easier

Members earn **20% bonus Tier Miles** on every Emirates and flydubai flight in the window, and Emirates says the Tier Miles required to renew or upgrade are reduced for the period — so Silver, Gold or Platinum status sits within closer reach for anyone flying through August.

## Partner earning lifts 50% — with a cap and a registration step

Skywards is adding 50% bonus Miles on hotel stays (Marriott Bonvoy, IHG, Jumeirah and Emirates Skywards Hotels), car rentals (Hertz, Avis & Budget, Europcar, Sixt, CarTrawler), partner-airline flights (United, Air Canada, Aegean, Japan Airlines) and Emirates Holidays packages. The catch: it is **capped at 10,000 bonus Miles per partner**, **registration is required**, and the bonus posts **within 60 days after the campaign ends** — not instantly.

## Reward-flight discount on easyJet and Jet2

Short-haul reward flights on the two European carriers drop to **4,500 Miles** from 9,000, across all routes, cabins and fares. Useful on a European trip; niche for most UAE-based members.

## The fine print that bites

Travel must be *completed* by 31 August 2026, not merely booked. For Emirates NBD Skywards co-brand cardholders, the tier-miles boost stacks on normal card earning, which makes the window a sensible time to put flight and partner spend on the card.
