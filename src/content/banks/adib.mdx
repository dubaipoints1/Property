Abu Dhabi Islamic Bank (ADIB) is one of the UAE's largest Islamic banks. Its cards are
Sharia-compliant covered cards, spanning a 4% household cashback line, Etihad Guest and
Emirates Skywards co-brands, and SHARE-points cards with Majid Al Futtaim.
