First Abu Dhabi Bank (FAB) is the largest bank in the UAE by assets and a major
issuer of UAE credit cards across every major loyalty currency — from cashback
to direct Skywards and Etihad Guest co-brands. FAB Rewards points transfer 1:1
to several airline partners, which makes the FAB lineup the strongest
transferable-points bank in the UAE.
