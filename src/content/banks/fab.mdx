First Abu Dhabi Bank (FAB) is the largest bank in the UAE by assets and a major
issuer of UAE credit cards across every major loyalty currency — from cashback
to a direct Etihad Guest co-brand. FAB Rewards transfer to several airline
partners, which makes the FAB lineup one of the strongest transferable-points
line-ups in the UAE.
