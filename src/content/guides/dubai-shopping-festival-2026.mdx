The **Dubai Shopping Festival** is the longest, loudest retail
window on the UAE calendar — roughly a month of stacked retailer
discounts, mall raffle draws, and bank-card spend promos. The
honest editorial read is that most of the headline "70% off"
banners are clearing old stock; the real edge is the
card-stacking and the raffles, not the rack price.

This guide is the playbook for the reader with a UAE credit card
and a shopping list, not the reader hoping a designer handbag
will appear at half price.

## DSF 2026 — the dates

DSF 2026 runs across roughly five weeks in mid-December 2025 to
late January 2026, with the headline weekend in the first week of
January. *Editor to verify exact 2026 dates against the official
mydsf.ae and visitdubai.com calendars before publish — the dates
shift slightly each edition and the closing raffle weekend is the
anchor for several bank promos.*

## What actually moves during DSF

Three things are worth your attention, in order:

- **Retailer cashback boosts.** Specific mall tenants run "spend
  AED 500, get an AED 100 mall voucher" or similar
  voucher-back promos. The voucher is restricted to the same mall
  group (Emaar malls vs Majid Al Futtaim malls); read the small
  print before assuming it stacks with a card.
- **Mall raffle draws.** Both Emaar (Dubai Mall) and Majid Al
  Futtaim (Mall of the Emirates, City Centre) run prize draws
  during DSF — typically one raffle ticket per AED 200–500
  spent. The headline prizes are cars and gold; the realistic
  outcome is the entry, not the win.
- **Bank-card spend promos.** UAE issuers run DSF-tied campaigns
  every year — "spend AED 5,000 on your FAB card during DSF, get
  AED 250 cashback" or similar. The campaigns are timed to the
  festival but the spend thresholds are set by the bank, not the
  mall. *Editor to verify each issuer's current DSF campaign
  microsite at the start of December — terms refresh annually.*

## The card-stacking pattern

A clean DSF transaction stacks three layers without breaking any
single programme's terms:

1. **The retailer's own DSF promo.** Whatever the store window
   advertises — buy-one-get-one, percentage off, voucher-back.
2. **The mall raffle entry.** Spend over the raffle threshold on
   a single receipt; keep the receipt and register it at the
   mall's customer-service desk or in the mall app.
3. **The card spend.** Pay on the UAE card whose category-earn
   rate matches the spend (cashback on a cashback card, mall
   points on a co-brand card, airline miles on an airline card).

The fourth layer — issuer-specific DSF spend promos — applies on
top if your card is in the campaign. Treat it as a bonus, not the
base case.

## Which card category fits which mall

- **Dubai Mall and Mall of the Emirates (Emaar / MAF anchor
  tenants).** A flat-rate UAE cashback card (FAB Cashback or
  equivalent) is the lowest-friction choice. The earn shows up
  on the statement regardless of which tenant rings the sale.
- **Co-brand mall cards.** The Emirates NBD U by Emaar range
  earns U By Emaar points specifically inside Emaar properties
  (Dubai Mall, Dubai Hills Mall, Address hotels). If your DSF
  spend is concentrated in Emaar venues, this is the
  highest-earn option. *Editor to verify the current U by Emaar
  earn rate and any DSF multiplier against the issuer's site.*
- **Grocery and household at LuLu / Carrefour.** The ENBD LuLu
  24/7 co-brand earns LuLu points faster than a generic card;
  for Carrefour spend, a cashback card is typically the better
  match unless MAF runs a card-specific campaign.

## What not to do

- **Do not open a new card mid-DSF to chase a sign-up bonus.** A
  hard AECB enquiry inside the festival window, then a flurry of
  retail spend, reads to underwriters as the exact distress
  pattern the bureau is built to flag.
- **Do not assume the raffle voucher is fungible.** Emaar
  vouchers spend at Emaar properties; MAF vouchers spend at MAF.
  Crossing them is the most common shopper-frustration of the
  whole festival.
- **Do not chase the headline 70%-off banner without a baseline
  price.** A noticeable share of "DSF-only" prices are the same
  prices the retailer ran in the prior month's mid-season sale.

## What to do during DSF 2026

1. Make a written shopping list before 1 December. Anything not
   on the list is an impulse buy that DSF was designed to extract.
2. Match each line item to the right card category (cashback,
   co-brand, airline) before you walk into the mall.
3. Pull each of your issuers' DSF campaign pages on the morning
   the festival opens; opt in where opt-in is required.
4. Register for the Emaar and MAF mall raffles in the respective
   apps before your first transaction.
