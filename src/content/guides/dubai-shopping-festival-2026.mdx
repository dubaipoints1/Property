The **Dubai Shopping Festival** is roughly a month of stacked
retailer discounts, mall raffle draws, and bank-card spend
promos. The honest editorial read is that most of the headline
"70% off" banners are clearing old stock; the real edge is the
card-stacking and the raffles, not the rack price.

## DSF 2026 — the dates

DSF 2026 runs across roughly five weeks in mid-December 2025 to
late January 2026, with the headline weekend in the first week
of January. *Editor to verify exact 2026 dates against mydsf.ae
and visitdubai.com before publish — the dates shift each edition
and the closing raffle weekend is the anchor for several bank
promos.*

## What actually moves during DSF

Three things, in order:

- **Retailer voucher-back promos.** "Spend AED 500, get an AED
  100 mall voucher" — restricted to the same mall group (Emaar
  vs Majid Al Futtaim). Read the small print.
- **Mall raffle draws.** Both Emaar (Dubai Mall) and MAF (Mall
  of the Emirates, City Centre) run prize draws — typically one
  raffle ticket per AED 200–500 spent. The headline prizes are
  cars and gold; the realistic outcome is the entry, not the win.
- **Bank-card spend promos.** UAE issuers run DSF-tied campaigns
  every year — "spend AED 5,000 on your card, get AED 250
  cashback" or similar. *Editor to verify each issuer's current
  DSF campaign microsite at the start of December — terms refresh
  annually.*

## The card-stacking pattern

A clean DSF transaction stacks three layers:

1. **The retailer's own DSF promo** — buy-one-get-one,
   percentage off, voucher-back.
2. **The mall raffle entry.** Spend over the raffle threshold
   on a single receipt; register it in the mall app.
3. **The card spend.** Pay on the UAE card whose category-earn
   rate matches the spend.

Issuer-specific DSF spend promos apply on top if your card is in
the campaign — treat as a bonus, not the base case.

## Which card category fits which mall

- **Dubai Mall / Mall of the Emirates.** A flat-rate UAE
  cashback card (FAB Cashback or equivalent) is the
  lowest-friction choice — the earn shows up regardless of which
  tenant rings the sale.
- **Co-brand mall cards.** The Emirates NBD U by Emaar range
  earns points inside Emaar properties (Dubai Mall, Dubai Hills
  Mall, Address hotels). If your DSF spend is concentrated in
  Emaar venues, this is the highest-earn option. *Editor to
  verify the current U by Emaar earn rate and any DSF
  multiplier against the issuer's site.*
- **Grocery and household.** The ENBD LuLu 24/7 co-brand earns
  LuLu points faster than a generic card; for Carrefour, a
  cashback card is usually the better match.

## What not to do

- **Do not open a new card mid-DSF to chase a sign-up bonus.** A
  hard AECB enquiry inside the festival window, then a flurry of
  retail spend, reads to underwriters as a distress pattern.
- **Do not assume the raffle voucher is fungible.** Emaar
  vouchers spend at Emaar properties; MAF vouchers spend at MAF.
- **Do not chase the 70%-off banner without a baseline price.** A
  noticeable share of "DSF-only" prices match the retailer's
  prior-month mid-season sale.

## What to do during DSF 2026

1. Make a written shopping list before 1 December.
2. Match each line item to the right card category (cashback,
   co-brand, airline) before you walk into the mall.
3. Pull each issuer's DSF campaign page on opening morning; opt
   in where opt-in is required.
4. Register for the Emaar and MAF mall raffles in their apps
   before your first transaction.
