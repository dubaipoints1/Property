## Minimum salary

UAE banks set a **monthly minimum salary** for each card tier. For premium
travel cards, expect AED 25,000–30,000 per month; for entry-level cashback
cards, AED 5,000–8,000 is common.

## Salary transfer

Some banks **require** your salary to be transferred to an account with them
before approving certain cards. Others treat salary transfer as optional but
weight it heavily in approval decisions.

## Residency

A valid **UAE residence visa** and Emirates ID are required for almost all UAE
credit cards. Tourist applications are not accepted.

## Worked example

The Emirates NBD Skywards Infinite requires AED 30,000 monthly income, does
not strictly require salary transfer, and does require UAE residency.
