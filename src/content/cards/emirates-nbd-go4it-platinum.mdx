The Go4it Platinum is Emirates NBD's lifestyle card built around Dubai's entertainment and transit
ecosystem. It doubles as a Nol card, making it uniquely practical for daily Dubai commuters, and
offers VOX Cinemas BOGO plus RTA integration perks.

## Earn rates

Go4it cards earn **Plus Points**. Points can be redeemed for cashback or converted to Skywards Miles.

| Spend type | Plus Points per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Weekends | **0.025** (5 PP per AED 200) |
| RTA (transit, parking) | **0.02** (4 PP per AED 200) |
| Standard (weekday retail) | **0.01** (1 PP per AED 100) |

Plus Points conversion: 25 Plus Points = 1 Skywards Mile (approx 0.4 fils per AED spent as miles).

## Nol card integration

The Go4it Platinum chip works directly as a Nol card — tap to pay for:
- Dubai Metro (including Gold Class)
- RTA buses and water buses
- RTA parking meters
- Dubai Ferry (free ride benefit for cardholder + 1 guest)

## Go4it Gold vs Platinum comparison

| Feature | Gold (AED 104) | Platinum (AED 209) |
|---|---|---|
| Weekend PP earn | 5 PP per AED 400 | 5 PP per AED 200 |
| Life insurance | AED 75,000 | AED 100,000 |
| Metro access | Standard | Gold Class |
| VOX BOGO | — | Yes |

## Notes

- Annual fee is AED 208.95 (inclusive of VAT).
- Highest-value perk is VOX BOGO — worth up to ~AED 120/month if you watch 2–3 films.
- Best for Dubai residents who rely on RTA transit and regularly visit malls/cinemas.
