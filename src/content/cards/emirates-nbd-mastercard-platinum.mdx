The Emirates NBD Mastercard Platinum is a no-fee general-purpose card with a
modest Plus Points structure and the standard Mastercard Platinum benefit set. AED 0
annual fee, 1.99% FX fee, and a Plus Points earn structure.

## Earn rates

| Spend category | % back as Plus Points |
|---|---|
| General spend | **1.5%** |
| Groceries | **0.4%** |
| Fuel & utilities | **0.2%** |

Plus Points convert to Skywards Miles, cashback, or merchant credit at ENBD's
published rates (~1¢ per point at the cashback ceiling).

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 0 |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 12,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not required |

## Key conditions to know

**Mastercard Platinum benefits:**
- Purchase protection (verify cover and exclusions in the issuer
  certificate)
- Travel inconvenience cover (flight delay, baggage)
- Mastercard Priceless offers — regional promotions, varies by month
- Limited concierge support via the Mastercard Platinum desk

**Fuel category definition:**
- MCC 5541 (service stations) and 5542 (automated fuel dispensers) qualify
- ADNOC, ENOC, and EPPCO transactions typically code correctly; verify
  with small test transactions if a specific provider is critical

## Best for

UAE residents on AED 12,000+ a month who want a no-fee Mastercard as a
backup or secondary card.

## Watch out for

- **0.2% fuel & utilities is not market-leading.** RAKBANK and ADCB run specialist fuel
  cards with higher multipliers; if fuel is your top category, look there
- **1.5% general earn** is unremarkable — pair this card with a cashback or
  category card for groceries and dining
- **No welcome bonus** published at our last verification
- **Plus Points realisation** is uneven; redeem as cashback rather than
  transferring to Skywards at the internal conversion rate
