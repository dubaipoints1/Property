The Emirates NBD U by Emaar Family is the no-fee tier of ENBD's U by Emaar Visa
co-brand. It earns UPoints redeemable across the Emaar Group's UAE properties —
Dubai Mall, Dubai Marina Mall, Dubai Hills Mall, Address, Rove and Vida hotels,
Reel Cinemas, Emaar Entertainment venues, and Emaar F&B — with a 5% headline
rate at those merchants and a thin 1% base elsewhere.

<HotTip>
Fuel, groceries and utilities earn **less** than the 1% base on this card —
0.10–0.25% — because ENBD applies a category de-emphasis at those merchants
across the U by Emaar Family tier. Run your real spend through the rates before
applying: a household whose card spend is mostly groceries and fuel will earn
materially less than on any flat-rate cashback card, even at a zero annual fee.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-u-by-emaar-family" />

No annual fee, no joining fee, no salary-transfer requirement. The
foreign-currency fee is **1.99%** on every non-AED transaction — the UAE
standard, not a saving. Interest is 3.49% per month (about 42% APR) if you
revolve a balance; minimum payment 5% of the outstanding or AED 100, whichever
is higher. Clear the statement in full and the interest is irrelevant.

## Watch out for

- **UPoints lock you into the Emaar ecosystem.** They redeem only at Emaar's
  2,500+ partner outlets — no airline transfers, no hotel-programme conversion,
  no clean cashback escape. If your weekend routine moves away from Dubai Mall,
  the rewards stop converting.
- **Fuel and groceries earn below base.** The category de-emphasis on fuel
  (0.10%), utilities (0.10%) and groceries (0.25%) is unusual — most no-fee
  cards earn at least the base rate on those. Households whose card spend
  concentrates there should pair this with a flat-rate cashback card or skip it
  outright.
- **The Emaar match has to be real.** "I'll go to Dubai Mall more" is not a
  reason to take this card. Run twelve months of your actual Emaar spend
  (mall purchases, restaurants, hotel stays, cinema tickets) and confirm the
  5% rate returns meaningful UPoints before applying.
- **Welcome bonus unclear.** Unlike the Infinite tier, the Family card does not
  carry a separately published welcome offer at our last verification.

## Bottom line

A focused no-fee earner for households whose weekend lives at Emaar venues —
Dubai Mall regulars, Address-brunch loyalists, families routing entertainment through
Reel Cinemas and KidZania. The 5% UPoints rate on Emaar venues is the strongest no-fee
rate in that ecosystem and the AED 5,000 salary bar keeps it accessible to younger
households building a first card portfolio. **Apply** if your Dubai Mall and Address
spend is real and recurring. **Skip** if your spend pattern sits outside the Emaar list
— the category de-emphasis on fuel (0.10%), utilities (0.10%) and groceries (0.25%)
makes this an expensive everyday card even at a zero annual fee, and a no-fee flat-rate
cashback card returns more on the same household budget.
