The Emirates NBD U by Emaar Family is the no-fee tier of the U by Emaar
co-brand. It earns U Points redeemable across the Emaar Group's UAE
properties — Dubai Mall, Address Hotels and Resorts, Vida and Rove hotels,
Reel Cinemas, KidZania and Emaar restaurants.

## What you actually get

Earn rates are 10 U Points per AED 1 on fuel, 5 per AED 1 on dining,
5 per AED 1 on shopping, and 1 per AED 1 on everything else. Annual fee
is zero. FX fee is 0%. Salary transfer is not required; minimum monthly
salary AED 5,000. U Points redeem at participating Emaar outlets at a
published conversion ratio — practically, this works like a closed-loop
voucher currency rather than open cashback.

## Who should open it

The card matches a family that lives or shops in the Downtown / Dubai
Hills / Dubai Marina corridor — anywhere the household's weekend routine
already includes Emaar Malls properties, Reel Cinemas trips, or stays at
Address Hotels and Resorts. AED 5,000 minimum salary keeps this accessible
to younger households building their first card portfolio.

## Where it falls short

U Points are a closed-loop currency. The earn rates are strong but the
redemption value depends entirely on your willingness to spend at Emaar
outlets — which, for some households, means switching consumption patterns
just to liquidate the points. There is no clean cashback or statement-credit
escape hatch.

The right card for the Downtown / Dubai Hills household; the wrong one
for everyone else.
