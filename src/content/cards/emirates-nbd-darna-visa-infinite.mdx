The Darna Visa Infinite sits at the top of Emirates NBD's Darna programme stack.
Premium Visa Infinite benefits — concierge, travel insurance, global lounge access
— combined with Darna Points that redeem against Dubai property bills at
participating developers.

## Earn rates

| Spend category | Darna Points per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Everything else | **1** |

Earn is flat — there's no category multiplier. The card's value sits in the
Visa Infinite perks and the Darna redemption path, not in points generation
on everyday spending.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 1,575 |
| Published waiver threshold | None |
| FX fee | 0% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 30,000 |
| Salary transfer | **Required** |

The **0% FX fee on an Infinite-tier card** is genuinely rare in the UAE. If your
non-AED spend exceeds ~AED 80,000 a year, the FX saving alone (~AED 1,600 at a
typical 2% market FX fee) recovers the annual fee — even before any Darna
redemptions.

## Key conditions to know

**Salary transfer requirement:**
- Salary transfer to Emirates NBD is **mandatory** for approval
- The [salary-transfer tracker](/salary-transfer/) carries current
  switching-bonus offers — if you're already moving, time the bonus
  alongside this application

**Visa Infinite benefits:**
- Concierge service via Visa
- Visa Infinite travel insurance (verify cover limits in the certificate)
- Global lounge access (confirm provider and visit cap with the issuer)

**Darna redemption mechanics:**
- Points credited monthly to your Darna account
- Redeem against rent, service charges, or developer payments at
  participating Dubai property providers
- Confirm your landlord or developer is in the Darna network before applying —
  scope is narrower than open cashback

## Best for

High-income Dubai residents (AED 30,000+/month) who pay rent or service
charges to a Darna network participant and want to convert spend into housing
credit. Also strong for premium spenders with heavy international card use,
given the 0% FX fee.

## Watch out for

- **AED 1,575 annual fee with no published waiver.** Confirm whether ENBD
  offers a discretionary waiver for relationship customers before applying
- **Welcome bonus terms** are inconsistently documented across ENBD's
  marketing material at our last verification — confirm the current offer
  directly with the issuer
- **Salary-transfer lock-in.** Mandatory salary transfer makes future bank
  switches harder; check the rest of your banking relationship is durable
- **Flat 1% earn.** For everyday spend, the
  [SHARE Visa Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite/) or
  [Skywards Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-skywards-infinite/) earn more
  per dirham at the same fee band
