The Emirates NBD Darna Visa Infinite is the top tier of ENBD's Darna property-and-lifestyle
co-brand. AED 1,575 inclusive of VAT, AED 30,000 minimum salary, salary transfer required,
and 10% back as Darna Points at Aldar destinations — redeemable against rent
and service charges in Aldar communities, and dining and leisure at providers inside the network — plus
automatic Darna Platinum tier and the Visa Infinite benefit suite.

<HotTip>
On a 10% headline, the AED 1,575 annual fee breaks even at roughly **AED 1,315 a month** of
qualifying partner spend (AED 1,575 ÷ 10% ÷ 12). The fairer comparison is against the no-fee
[Darna Select](/cards/emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa/) at 6.25%: on the 3.75-point
differential, the fee is recovered at roughly **AED 3,500 a month** of partner spend. Run a
twelve-month partner-spend audit before applying: restaurants, mall purchases, hotel stays,
cinema. Anything below that line, and the no-fee Select earns the loyalty side without the
AED 1,575.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-darna-visa-infinite" />

The card carries an **AED 1,575 annual fee inclusive of VAT** with **no published waiver
threshold** — there is no spend-based escape from the fee. Salary transfer to Emirates NBD
is **mandatory** for approval. The **foreign-currency fee is 1.99%** — older marketing
summaries that described this as a 0% FX card are wrong against the current Schedule of
Charges. As with any rewards card, clear the statement in full each month — the standard
ENBD revolving rate will erase any Darna value many times over.

## What you get — per-benefit value

Darna publishes its conversion — 10 Darna Points = AED 1 — so the earn carries honest AED
figures, with the standing caveat that the value only exists inside the Darna partner
network.

### 10% back at Darna partners

The strongest partner rate in the Darna stack. At AED 3,000 a month of genuine partner
spend, **Value to me: AED 3,600/year** in Darna Points value; at AED 8,000 a month,
**AED 9,600/year**. On earn alone, the AED 1,575 fee is recovered by roughly
**AED 15,750 of qualifying partner spend a year — about AED 1,315 a month**. The fairer
hurdle is the marginal one: against holding the free
[Darna Select](/cards/emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa/) at 6.25% instead, the 3.75-point
differential recovers the fee at about **AED 42,000 of partner spend a year — AED 3,500 a
month**.

### 1.5% on everything else

AED 10,000 a month of non-partner spend returns **Value to me: AED 1,800/year** in
network-locked points — paid in a currency with no exit ramp, where a flat cashback card
pays cash on the same spend.

### Welcome bonus + Platinum tier + Visa Infinite chrome

15,000 Darna Points (7,500 on joining, 7,500 on AED 20,000 of spend in 90 days) =
**AED 1,500** inside the network, plus the AED 500 YOUGotaGift voucher modelled at
**AED 400** under our standard 20% voucher discount — **Value to me: roughly AED 1,900,
one-time, year one**, which on its own clears the first year's AED 1,575 fee. Darna
Platinum tier has no published AED value. The Visa Infinite lounge access carries no
published provider or visit cap on the issuer page — verify both with ENBD before
pricing it above AED 0.

**Total package:** a genuine Darna household at AED 3,000–8,000 a month of partner spend
books roughly **AED 3,600–9,600/year** in Darna Points value against the AED 1,575 fee,
with ~AED 1,900 of one-time welcome value covering year one. Below about AED 3,500 a
month of partner spend, the free Darna Select plus a flat cashback card is the better
pairing.

## Watch out for

- **AED 3,500/month of partner spend is the honest break-even.** On the 10% rate alone
  the AED 1,575 fee is recovered at about AED 1,315/month — but measured against the
  no-fee Darna Select's 6.25%, the differential only repays the fee from roughly
  AED 3,500/month of partner spend. Below that, you are paying for Platinum tier and
  Visa Infinite chrome. Confirm a twelve-month partner-spend total before applying.
- **Salary-transfer lock-in and clawback.** ENBD requires salary transfer to the bank for
  approval and applies a standard clawback if the account is closed inside the first
  twelve months — the welcome offer and tier status are recovered, and the AED 1,575 fee
  is not refunded. If you may leave the UAE or switch banks within the year, hold off.
- **No airline or hotel transfers on Darna Points.** They redeem at Aldar's partner
  network and nowhere else. There is no points-to-Skywards or points-to-Bonvoy escape
  valve — hold this for genuine Aldar spend, not as an open-ended rewards card.
- **1.99% FX, not 0%.** Legacy marketing summaries describing this as a zero-FX card are
  out of date against the current Schedule of Charges. Not the right card for material
  international spend.
- **Better Infinite alternatives if you are not an Aldar household.** The 1.5% on
  non-partner spend is thin for the Visa Infinite tier. The
  [Skywards Visa Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-skywards-infinite/) or the
  [SHARE Visa Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite/) earn more per dirham on
  general spend at the same fee band.

## Bottom line

For the household whose discretionary spend genuinely lives inside the partner footprint
at AED 3,500+ a month — Yas Mall regulars, Yas Island season-pass holders, families
routing dining and weekend entertainment through the Aldar list — the 10% Darna Points
rate is the strongest in the Darna stack, the welcome is meaningful, and Platinum tier
plus Visa Infinite chrome stack on top. The AED 1,575 fee earns its keep.

For everyone else it's the wrong Visa Infinite: non-Aldar spend earns 1.5%, Darna Points
lock into the ecosystem, the salary transfer adds friction, and the 1.99% FX rules it out
on overseas spend. **Apply** if your year of partner spend clears roughly AED 42,000 and
you accept the salary-transfer commitment; **skip** otherwise — a no-fee
[Darna Select](/cards/emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa/) plus a broad cashback card captures
the loyalty side without the AED 1,575.
