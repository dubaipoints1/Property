## Cashback

Cashback is paid monthly into the card. The accelerated rates apply only when you spend
at least AED 3,000 in the month, and each category is capped:

<EarnRateTable card="hsbc-live-plus" />

<HotTip>
Two conditions shape the cashback. First, you must spend at least **AED 3,000** in a
month, or the bonus categories drop to the 0.5% base. Second, each bonus bucket is
capped at **AED 200** per statement cycle — **6% dining**, **5% fuel**, and **2%
groceries and entertainment combined** — so the most you can earn on the accelerated
categories is **AED 600 a month**. The 6% dining rate therefore tops out at about AED
3,300 of restaurant spend.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="hsbc-live-plus" />

There's no annual fee in the first year, then **AED 313.95** (incl. VAT) — waived if you
spend at least **AED 12,000** in the prior 12 months. The foreign-currency fee is **2%**,
notably below the 2.5–3% most UAE cards charge, which makes Live+ a reasonable travel-spend
card too. The fixed APR is 44.28% — relevant only if you carry a balance.

## Welcome cashback breakdown

<WelcomeBonusCallout card="hsbc-live-plus" />

New credit-card customers could earn **up to AED 1,500** in welcome cashback through the
May 2026 cycle (which closed 31 May 2026) on meeting the spend and funding requirements,
plus **AED 100** for the first mobile-wallet payment. You can't have held an HSBC primary
card in the prior six months. HSBC's welcome cycles refresh periodically — confirm the
current welcome amount and the qualifying spend before applying.

## Watch out for

- **The AED 3,000 gate and AED 200 caps.** The headline rates suit moderate, spread
  spend — a single big category maxes its AED 200 cap fast, and a quiet month drops you
  to 0.5%.
- **0.5% base is weak.** Spend outside dining, fuel, groceries and entertainment earns
  almost nothing — pair Live+ with a flat-rate card for the rest.
- **Higher salary bar off-panel.** AED 5,000 only applies at HSBC-approved employers;
  otherwise it's AED 12,500.
- **Lounge condition.** You must make a small international purchase each year to keep
  the complimentary lounge visits.

## Is it worth it?

For a UAE resident who spends across dining, fuel and groceries and travels a few times
a year, Live+ is one of the better mainstream picks: high (if capped) category cashback,
a low 2% FX fee, no first-year fee, and a perk stack — Careem Plus, Zomato Gold, lounge
access, travel insurance — that many fee-charging cards don't match. New arrivals get a
genuine head start through HSBC's international credit-history sharing.

It's a weaker fit for heavy single-category spenders (the caps bite) or anyone whose
spend sits mostly outside the bonus categories (0.5% base). As ever with a cashback card,
the value assumes you clear the balance in full each month.

## Bottom line

A strong everyday-and-travel cashback all-rounder with a low FX fee and real expat
onboarding. **Apply** if your spend spreads across the bonus categories and you value the
perks and FX rate. **Skip** if you spend heavily in one category or mostly off-category.
