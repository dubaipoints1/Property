Launched in May 2025, the SHARE Visa Signature is Emirates NBD's co-branded card with Majid Al
Futtaim — the group behind Mall of the Emirates, City Centre Malls, Carrefour, VOX Cinemas, and
more. It earns 6% back in SHARE Points across the entire MAF ecosystem and is completely free
for life with no annual or joining fee.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are **% back in SHARE Points** (1 SHARE Point = AED 0.10 in redemption value at MAF outlets):

| Spend category | Rate |
|---|---|
| All MAF brands & outlets | **6%** |
| All other local & international spend | **1%** |

MAF brands covered: Mall of the Emirates, all City Centre Malls (Deira, Mirdif, Me'aisem, Sharjah,
Fujairah, Bahrain), Carrefour, VOX Cinemas, Magic Planet, Ski Dubai, iFly Dubai, and 5,000+ stores
within MAF-managed destinations.

## SHARE Points value

SHARE Points earned via this card are credited to your Majid Al Futtaim SHARE loyalty account:
- Redeemable instantly at 5,000+ MAF stores and partner outlets
- 1 SHARE Point ≈ AED 0.10 (verify redemption rate on SHARE app/website)
- Points are **not transferable to airlines or hotel programs**

## Card tiers

| Feature | Signature (Free) | Infinite (AED 1,500) |
|---|---|---|
| MAF earn rate | **6%** | **8%** |
| Non-MAF earn rate | 1% | 1–2% |
| Min salary | AED 12,000 | AED 30,000 |
| Annual fee | Free for life | AED 1,500 (waived on AED 40,000 spend in 3 months) |
| Lounge access | 1,000+ via Visa Airport Companion | Unlimited |

## Best for

UAE residents who regularly shop at Carrefour, visit City Centre Malls or Mall of the Emirates,
or use VOX Cinemas. The 6% return at Carrefour alone can deliver strong value for families —
AED 5,000/month grocery spend = AED 300 back/month.

## Watch out for

- SHARE Points only redeem within MAF ecosystem — no flexibility beyond MAF brands
- Welcome bonus requires AED 25,000 spend in 3 months (~AED 8,333/month) — requires high spenders
- Non-MAF earn at 1% is below market-leading cashback cards for general use
