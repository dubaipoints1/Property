## Earn rates

All earn rates are credited as **% back in SHARE Points** (10 SHARE Points = AED 1 in
redemption value at MAF outlets):

<EarnRateTable card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature" />

Partner brands here means the full Majid Al Futtaim footprint — Mall of the Emirates,
all City Centre malls (Deira, Mirdif, Me'aisem, Sharjah, Fujairah and beyond), Carrefour
in-store and online, VOX Cinemas, Magic Planet, Ski Dubai, iFly Dubai, and 5,000+ stores
at MAF-managed destinations.

<HotTip>
The 6% rate is paid as SHARE Points (10 points = AED 1). AED 5,000 a month at Carrefour
returns 3,000 SHARE Points worth AED 300 — and with no annual fee to recover, every
dirham is yield. Stack it with a flat-rate cashback card for spend outside MAF.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature" />

Free for life means free for life — no joining fee, no annual fee, no spend gate.
FX is 1.99% and the monthly interest rate is 3.25% (~39% APR); all fees include 5% VAT.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

<WelcomeBonusCallout card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature" />

The 5,000 SHARE Points are worth AED 500 of MAF spend; the spend threshold is
AED 25,000 in 90 days, which works out to ~AED 8,300 a month. ENBD has also been
running an AED 350 YOUGotaGift HappyYOU Card on online applications; verify it's
still live on the application page.

## SHARE Signature vs SHARE Infinite

<CardComparison cards={["emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature", "emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite"]} />

The Signature gives up 2 percentage points on the MAF rate (6% vs 8%) and downgrades
from unlimited lounge access to capped Visa Airport Companion visits. In exchange it
costs zero a year and asks for an AED 12,000 salary instead of AED 30,000. **The
decision rule:** if your annual MAF spend is under ~AED 75,000 a year, the Signature
nets out ahead even before counting the AED 1,575 fee you avoid.

## Watch out for

- **SHARE Points are closed-loop.** Useful only at MAF — no airline, hotel, or cash
  redemption flexibility. If your loyalty drifts away from the ecosystem, the points strand.
- **Welcome bonus needs ~AED 8,300/month.** AED 25,000 in 90 days is achievable on
  rent-card or school-fee spend but not casual swipes.
- **Non-MAF base earn is 1%.** Below market-leading cashback cards for general use —
  pair with a flat-rate cashback or miles card.
- **Groceries outside Carrefour earn 0.25%; fuel and utilities 0.10%.** Treat as a
  category card, not an everywhere card.

## Bottom line

For a free-for-life card with a 6% category rate inside the largest retail ecosystem
in the UAE, the Signature is hard to argue with. A household spending AED 5,000 a month
at Carrefour earns AED 300 of SHARE value back; add a weekly VOX trip and a few City
Centre swipes and yield clears AED 500 a month at no cost. The Visa Airport Companion
lounge access — at 1,000+ lounges, on a no-fee card — is the kind of perk usually
gated to AED 1,000-fee tiers.

**Apply** if you shop Carrefour, City Centre malls or VOX with any regularity. **Skip**
if your spend is entirely outside MAF; the 1% non-MAF base means you shouldn't put
general spend on this card anyway. Pair it with a flat-cashback card like the
[CBD One](/cards/cbd-one/) for everything off-ecosystem, or with the
[Skywards Signature](/cards/emirates-nbd-skywards-signature/) if you fly Emirates.
