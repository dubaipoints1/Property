## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite" />

The headline numbers: an AED 1,500 joining fee in year one (refunded on AED 40,000 spend
in 90 days), and an AED 1,575 annual fee from year two with **no published spend-based
waiver**. FX is 1.99%, the monthly interest rate is 3.25% (~39% APR), and all fees include
5% VAT.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

<WelcomeBonusCallout card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite" />

Trigger threshold is the same AED 40,000 spend in 90 days that refunds the joining fee
— so a household that clears it once gets both the AED 1,500 fee refund and 10,000
SHARE Points (AED 1,000) at the same time. ENBD has also been running an AED 500
YOUGotaGift HappyYOU Card top-up on online applications; verify on the application
page that this is live before you apply.

## What you get — per-benefit value

SHARE Points carry a published redemption rate — 10 points = AED 1 at MAF
outlets — so unlike most closed-loop currencies we can price this card in
dirhams directly. The fees to beat: AED 1,500 joining in year one (refundable
on spend) and AED 1,575 annual from year two.

### Welcome bonus

10,000 SHARE Points on AED 40,000 of spend in the first 90 days.
**Value to me: AED 1,000, one-time** at the published 10-points-per-AED-1 rate —
spendable only inside MAF. The AED 500 YOUGotaGift card on online applications
is a campaign extra; count it only after confirming it's live on the
application page.

### Joining-fee refund

The AED 1,500 joining fee refunds on the same AED 40,000 / 90-day threshold.
**Value to me: AED 1,500, one-time** — an avoided cost rather than earn, but
real money. Clear the threshold and year one is effectively free.

### 8% earn across the MAF footprint

**Value to me: AED 4,800/year** on AED 5,000 a month of MAF spend (Carrefour,
City Centre, VOX and the rest); **AED 2,880/year** at AED 3,000 a month. Paid
as SHARE Points at face value when redeemed at an MAF till — the figure only
realises if you keep shopping the ecosystem.

### 1.5% on general spend

**Value to me: AED 540/year** on AED 3,000 a month of non-MAF spend — but a
flat 1%+ AED-cashback card pays similar value in open currency, so we'd route
this spend elsewhere and treat the line as AED 0 in practice.

### Unlimited Visa Airport Companion lounge access

Cardholder + 1 guest at 1,200+ lounges. At the AED 200 walk-in equivalent we
use across reviews:
**Value to me: AED 1,600/year** on four trips with a guest; **AED 800/year**
for a two-trip-a-year traveller flying solo with a companion.

### Golf, concierge, travel insurance

Up to 40% off green fees at 100+ courses plus complimentary rounds at select
UAE courses. We carry no published green-fee baseline to price the discount
against, and concierge and insurance only pay out when used.
**Value to me: AED 0 for most readers** — genuine upside for a regular golfer,
but utilisation-dependent and unpriced by design.

**Total package:** an MAF-heavy household (AED 5,000 a month in-ecosystem,
four flown trips with a guest) collects roughly **AED 8,900 in year one**
(welcome + fee refund + earn + lounge) against the AED 1,500 joining fee it
refunds — and about **AED 5,600–6,400/year ongoing** against the AED 1,575
renewal. At AED 2,000 a month of MAF spend the ongoing package thins to
roughly **AED 2,700/year** — still ahead of the fee, but the
[free-for-life Signature](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature/) likely
nets out better at that level.

## SHARE Infinite vs SHARE Signature

<CardComparison cards={["emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite", "emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature"]} />

The Infinite pays 2 percentage points more on the MAF rate (8% vs 6%) and adds
unlimited lounge access — but it costs AED 1,575 a year from year two with no waiver,
and the salary bar is more than double. **The decision rule:** if you'll spend more
than ~AED 75,000 a year inside MAF, the 2-point gap beats the renewal fee even with
the lounge perk taken at zero value. Below that, the Signature is the right call.

## Watch out for

- **The AED 1,575 annual fee from year two has no waiver.** Only the joining fee is
  refundable. Model year-two MAF spend before you apply.
- **SHARE Points are closed-loop.** Useful only at MAF — no airline, hotel, or cash
  redemption flexibility. If your loyalty drifts away from Carrefour and City Centre,
  the points strand.
- **1.5% on general spend is below average** for a card at this fee. Pair with a
  flat-rate cashback or miles card for non-MAF spend.
- **Groceries outside Carrefour earn just 0.375%**; fuel and utilities earn 0.15%.
  Treat this as a category card, not an everywhere card.

## Bottom line

For a UAE household whose monthly retail and dining spend lands inside Majid Al Futtaim
— Carrefour for groceries, City Centre or Mall of the Emirates for shopping, VOX for
cinema, Ski Dubai or iFly for family weekends — the Infinite returns more than any other
UAE card on this ecosystem. AED 5,000 a month at Carrefour alone is AED 400 of SHARE
value; add a weekly VOX trip and a few MAF mall swipes and the AED 1,575 renewal clears
inside four months. For frequent travellers, the unlimited Visa Airport Companion
access neutralises a further chunk of the fee — a comparable standalone subscription
runs AED 1,000+ a year.

**Apply** if household spend is MAF-heavy and you'll clear AED 40,000 in 90 days; pair
with a flat-cashback card like the [CBD One](/cards/cbd-one/) for everything else.
**Skip** if your spend is spread across non-MAF merchants — the
[Signature](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature/) is the same earn engine with no
fee, and 1.5% on general spend isn't enough to carry the AED 1,575 renewal.
