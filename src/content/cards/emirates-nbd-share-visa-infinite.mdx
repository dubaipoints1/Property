The SHARE Visa Infinite is Emirates NBD's premium co-branded card with Majid Al Futtaim.
Launched in May 2025, it delivers 8% back in SHARE Points across the MAF ecosystem — among the
highest category earn rates on any UAE card — alongside unlimited lounge access and a full Visa
Infinite benefits suite.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are **% back in SHARE Points**:

| Spend category | Rate |
|---|---|
| All MAF brands & outlets | **8%** |
| All other local & international spend | **1%** |

MAF brands: Mall of the Emirates, all City Centre Malls, Carrefour, VOX Cinemas, Magic Planet,
Ski Dubai, iFly Dubai, and 5,000+ stores at MAF-managed destinations.

## Fee structure

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (year 1) | AED 1,500 |
| Annual renewal fee | AED 1,500 |
| Fee refund condition | Spend AED 40,000 in first 3 months to recover joining fee |
| Monthly interest | 3.25% |
| FX fee | ~1.99% |

## SHARE vs U by Emaar Infinite — MAF ecosystem comparison

Both cards earn high rates at lifestyle destinations, but at different ecosystems:

| Feature | SHARE Infinite | U by Emaar Infinite |
|---|---|---|
| Top earn rate | 8% at MAF brands | 7.5% at Emaar properties |
| Ecosystem | City Centres, Carrefour, VOX | Dubai Mall, Address Hotels, Emaar F&B |
| Annual fee | AED 1,500 | AED 1,575 (+ AED 2,625 joining) |
| Lounge access | Unlimited Visa | Unlimited Visa |

## Best for

MAF loyalists — Carrefour shoppers, City Centre Mall regulars, and VOX Cinemas visitors who spend
heavily within the MAF ecosystem. Spending AED 5,000/month at Carrefour alone = AED 400 back/month
(8% of AED 5,000), easily covering the AED 1,500 annual fee within 4 months.

## Watch out for

- AED 1,500 joining fee payable upfront — refund requires AED 40,000 spend in 3 months
- SHARE Points only redeem within MAF ecosystem — no airline or hotel flexibility
- Non-MAF earn at 1% is low for an AED 1,500/year card
