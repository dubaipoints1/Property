## Earn rates

All earn rates are credited as **% back in SHARE Points** (10 SHARE Points = AED 1 in
redemption value at MAF outlets):

<EarnRateTable card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite" />

Partner brands here means the full Majid Al Futtaim footprint — Mall of the Emirates,
all City Centre malls (Deira, Mirdif, Me'aisem, Sharjah, Fujairah and beyond), Carrefour
in-store and online, VOX Cinemas, Magic Planet, Ski Dubai, iFly Dubai, and 5,000+ stores
at MAF-managed destinations.

<HotTip>
The 8% rate is paid as SHARE Points, not cashback — 10 points = AED 1 of redemption
value at MAF outlets. AED 5,000 a month at Carrefour returns 4,000 SHARE Points worth
AED 400, which clears the AED 1,575 renewal in under four months of MAF-heavy spend.
Points are closed-loop, so the value only realises if you actually shop the ecosystem.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite" />

The headline numbers: an AED 1,500 joining fee in year one (refunded on AED 40,000 spend
in 90 days), and an AED 1,575 annual fee from year two with **no published spend-based
waiver**. FX is 1.99%, the monthly interest rate is 3.25% (~39% APR), and all fees include
5% VAT.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

<WelcomeBonusCallout card="emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite" />

Trigger threshold is the same AED 40,000 spend in 90 days that refunds the joining fee
— so a household that clears it once gets both the AED 1,500 fee refund and 10,000
SHARE Points (AED 1,000) at the same time. ENBD has also been running an AED 500
YOUGotaGift HappyYOU Card top-up on online applications; verify on the application
page that this is live before you apply.

## SHARE Infinite vs SHARE Signature

<CardComparison cards={["emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite", "emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature"]} />

The Infinite pays 2 percentage points more on the MAF rate (8% vs 6%) and adds
unlimited lounge access — but it costs AED 1,575 a year from year two with no waiver,
and the salary bar is more than double. **The decision rule:** if you'll spend more
than ~AED 75,000 a year inside MAF, the 2-point gap beats the renewal fee even with
the lounge perk taken at zero value. Below that, the Signature is the right call.

## Watch out for

- **The AED 1,575 annual fee from year two has no waiver.** Only the joining fee is
  refundable. Model year-two MAF spend before you apply.
- **SHARE Points are closed-loop.** Useful only at MAF — no airline, hotel, or cash
  redemption flexibility. If your loyalty drifts away from Carrefour and City Centre,
  the points strand.
- **1.5% on general spend is below average** for a card at this fee. Pair with a
  flat-rate cashback or miles card for non-MAF spend.
- **Groceries outside Carrefour earn just 0.375%**; fuel and utilities earn 0.15%.
  Treat this as a category card, not an everywhere card.

## Is it worth it?

For a UAE household whose monthly retail and dining spend lands inside Majid Al Futtaim
— Carrefour for groceries, City Centre or Mall of the Emirates for shopping, VOX for
cinema, Ski Dubai or iFly for family weekends — the Infinite returns more than any other
UAE card on this ecosystem. AED 5,000 a month at Carrefour alone is AED 400 of SHARE
value; add a weekly VOX trip and a few MAF mall swipes and the AED 1,575 renewal clears
inside four months.

For a household that splits spending across non-MAF retail, the maths tips the other way.
The 1.5% base rate is below average, the AED 1,575 fee posts in full every year from
year two, and SHARE Points have no flexibility beyond MAF. The Signature is then the
better call — same ecosystem at a lower earn rate, but no fee and a lower salary bar.

For frequent travellers, the unlimited Visa Airport Companion access is genuinely
valuable — a separate Visa Airport Companion or Priority Pass subscription typically
costs AED 1,000+ a year, which neutralises a significant chunk of the renewal fee if
you'll actually use the lounges.

**Pay smart:** the best UAE card for this category is the
[SHARE Visa Infinite itself](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-infinite/) on MAF spend,
paired with a [flat-rate cashback card](/cards/cbd-one/) for the rest.

## Bottom line

The highest single-ecosystem earn rate on any UAE card, gated to a salary bar and a
spend pattern that has to actually live inside Majid Al Futtaim. **Apply** if your
household spend is MAF-heavy and you'll clear AED 40,000 in 90 days. **Skip** if your
spend is spread across non-MAF merchants — the [Signature](/cards/emirates-nbd-share-visa-signature/)
is the same earn engine with no fee.
