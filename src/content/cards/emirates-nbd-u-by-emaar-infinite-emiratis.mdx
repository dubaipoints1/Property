The Emirates NBD U by Emaar Infinite (for Emiratis) is a UAE-national-only
variant of the U by Emaar Infinite. It carries the Visa Infinite tier
benefits and a 25,000 U Points welcome bonus but a flat 1x ongoing earn
structure rather than the standard Infinite's 7.5%-back category rates.

## What you actually get

Earn is 1 U Point per AED 1 across all categories. Welcome offer is
25,000 U Points on activation, within a 90-day qualifying window. Annual
fee is AED 1,575. FX fee is 0%, which on a Visa Infinite is a meaningful
benefit. Salary transfer to Emirates NBD is required; minimum monthly
salary AED 30,000. Visa Infinite chrome applies — airport lounge access,
travel insurance, concierge — at the network-issued benefit level.

## Who should open it

The card matches UAE national customers earning AED 30,000+ a month who
already prefer Emirates NBD's national-segment service tier (Beyond /
Private Banking) and want a Visa Infinite from the bank without committing
to the structured-rewards focus of, say, the Skywards Infinite.

## Where it falls short

The 1x base earn on a card with a AED 1,575 annual fee is structurally
weak versus any of ENBD's category-bonus cards. If reward maximisation is
the priority, the standard U by Emaar Infinite (open to all residents at
the same fee) carries a 7.5%-back Emaar-venue rate this product appears
not to mirror. The Emiratis variant's value is in relationship benefits
and issuance preference, not earn rate.

A relationship card for ENBD's UAE-national priority segment; check the
non-Emiratis U by Emaar Infinite for sharper rewards mechanics.
