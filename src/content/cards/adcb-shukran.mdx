## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="adcb-shukran" />

Per the ADCB Schedule of Fees Ver.46 (February 2026), the annual fee is **AED 250
+ VAT (AED 262.50)** at the Aspire relationship tier, **with the first year
waived** for all UAE residents and the fee permanently waived for **UAE Nationals
at Excellency tier**. A non-National UAE resident pays AED 262.50 from year two;
a UAE National at Excellency holds the card without a recurring fee. The
nationality-and-tier conditional pricing is unusual in the UAE market and is
worth surfacing at application — confirm your relationship tier before assuming
the headline waiver applies. The cost to watch on either profile is the **2.99%
non-AED foreign-currency markup** per the same schedule — market-standard for
UAE Visas, no FX advantage on overseas spend. All published fees include 5% VAT.

## What you get — per-benefit value

ADCB quotes both the welcome bonus and the earn in AED-equivalent terms
("AED 1,000 in Shukrans", "10% back as Shukrans"), so we take Shukrans at
the AED face value ADCB itself states — redeemable only inside the Landmark
ecosystem. Counted against AED 0 in year one and AED 262.50 from year two.

### Welcome bonus

AED 1,000 in Shukrans for a UAE resident new to ADCB cards (AED 1,200 for
UAE Nationals; AED 200 for existing ADCB cardholders), on AED 8,000 of
spend inside 60 days.
**Value to me: AED 1,000, one-time** at the resident baseline — but only
spendable at Landmark brands, and reversed if you close the card inside
twelve months.

### 10% Landmark earn

10% back as Shukrans at Babyshop, Centrepoint, Emax, Homebox, Home Centre,
Lifestyle, Max, Shoemart, Splash and Styli.
**Value to me: AED 1,200/year** on AED 1,000 a month of Landmark spend;
**AED 600/year** at AED 500 a month. Taken at face when redeemed at a
Landmark till.

### 1.5% on other retail

**Value to me: AED 540/year** on AED 3,000 a month of general retail —
but paid in captive Shukrans, so a flat AED-cashback card at 1%+ is often
the better carrier for this spend.

### Reel Cinemas BOGO

Up to two complimentary tickets per month. At the AED 40–50 typical ticket:
**Value to me: AED 480–600/year** on one paired visit a month.

### Shukran Platinum tier fast-track

In-programme benefits ladder only — no published AED value.
**Value to me: not computable** — we don't book a figure on a status tier
without published per-benefit pricing.

**Total package:** a Landmark-loyal household at AED 1,000 a month of
Landmark spend plus a monthly cinema pairing collects roughly
**AED 2,700–2,800 in year one** (welcome included) against no fee, and
**AED 1,700–1,800/year ongoing** against the AED 262.50 renewal. Outside
the Landmark ecosystem the package collapses to the cinema line — and the
fee stops being worth paying.

## Watch out for

- **Year-two fee for residents.** AED 250 + VAT (AED 262.50) from year two onwards. UAE
  Nationals are exempt — make sure your application reflects the correct profile.
- **Existing-customer welcome collapses.** AED 200 for existing ADCB credit-card or
  covered-card holders versus the new-to-bank headline. The card is sharpest as a first-
  ADCB-card for a Landmark-loyal applicant.
- **Captive currency.** Shukrans redeem only at Landmark brands in the UAE. Useful if
  your household lives there; useless if not.
- **Welcome bonus is tiered by profile.** AED 1,200 only applies to UAE
  Nationals with no existing ADCB credit or covered card; other new-to-ADCB
  residents receive AED 1,000, and existing ADCB cardholders AED 200. Make
  sure your application reflects the profile you are claiming.
- **Clawback realities.** Shukran welcome bonuses are typically reversed if
  the card is closed within twelve months of issuance. The year-two
  AED 262.50 is not pro-rated for a mid-year cancellation — close before
  the renewal anniversary if your spend pattern has drifted away from the
  Landmark ecosystem.

## Is it worth it?

For a household that already shops the Landmark portfolio — Centrepoint for adult
wardrobe basics, Babyshop and Splash for kids, Home Centre and Homebox for the apartment,
Emax for electronics — the 10% Shukrans-back on spend you were going to do anyway is
straightforwardly strong. Add the Reel BOGO and the Shukran Platinum tier fast-track and
the year-one maths is clean even before the welcome bonus is calculated. UAE Nationals
hold the card indefinitely for the same reasoning.

For a resident whose retail spend is mostly outside the Landmark ecosystem, year one is
fine but year two onwards the AED 262.50 fee starts costing more than the captive
Shukrans return. In that case it is a year-one product to harvest the welcome and the
year-one earn, then close.

## Bottom line

A clean Landmark-Group co-brand whose maths is sharp for households shopping Centrepoint,
Babyshop, Splash, Home Centre and the rest — free at Excellency tier for UAE Nationals,
year one free for all other residents per SoF Ver.46. **Apply** if Landmark brands are
part of your monthly retail rotation. **Skip** in year two as a resident if your spend
has drifted elsewhere.
