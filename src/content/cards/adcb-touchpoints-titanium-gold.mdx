<HotTip>
The 5,000 bonus ADCB TouchPoints awarded on AED 5,000 of monthly card spend is
the single most valuable mechanic on this card. At the base earn of 1 point
per AED 1, AED 5,000 of spend earns 5,000 ADCB TouchPoints — and the bonus
doubles that effective rate to 2 points per AED 1 on the qualifying month.
Drop below AED 5,000 in any month and the bonus doesn't pay; concentrate
discretionary spend through the card to clear the gate each statement.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="adcb-touchpoints-titanium-gold" />

The card is free for life on the L2 schedule — no annual fee, no joining fee.
The FX fee is **2.99%** per the ADCB Schedule of Fees Ver.46 (February 2026)
— in line with the typical UAE 2.49–3.5% band, no FX advantage on overseas
spend. Minimum salary is **AED 5,000 a month** and salary transfer is not
required.

## Watch out for

- **The AED 5,000 spend gate.** The monthly bonus does the heavy lifting on
  this card. Drop below AED 5,000 in any statement month and the bonus
  doesn't pay — and the headline-effective earn rate halves.
- **No lounge or travel perks** on the issuer free-text. This is a domestic
  ADCB TouchPoints earner, not a travel card. Don't apply expecting Visa
  Airport Companion access without verifying it on the current product page.
- **TouchPoints exchange ratio.** ADCB TouchPoints exchange into Emirates
  Skywards Miles per the perks list, but the ratio is not in L2 — the value
  of the airline route is uncomputable until the conversion rate is verified.
- **Step-up at sustained AED 10,000+ monthly spend.** The Platinum and
  Betaqti tiers earn more per dirham and unlock anniversary bonus mechanics
  the Titanium Gold does not. At five-figure monthly spend, the step up
  pays back fast.

## Is it worth it?

For a reader at the AED 5,000–10,000 salary band who wants a free-for-life
ADCB TouchPoints earner, will reliably clear the AED 5,000 monthly spend
gate, and values in-store redemption at the LuLu/Carrefour/ADNOC partner
network over an airline programme: the TouchPoints Titanium Gold is the right
entry. The 2.99% FX fee is market-standard for UAE Visas — no advantage on
overseas spend, but no penalty either; take a dedicated travel card abroad
if international spend is material.

For a reader running household spend through one card and clearing AED 10,000
or more per month, the step up to a Platinum or Betaqti TouchPoints tier
earns more per dirham on the same volume — and at sustained spend, the
anniversary bonus mechanic on those higher tiers more than covers the AED 0
to AED 2,100 fee gap. Match the tier to the spend pattern, not the marketing.

## Bottom line

A free-for-life ADCB TouchPoints earner at the AED 5,000 salary bar, with a
fuel-category lift and a monthly bonus-point mechanic that doubles the
effective rate on qualifying months. **Apply** if you want a simple
TouchPoints-currency entry with no annual-fee risk. **Skip** if you'll run
AED 10,000+ monthly spend — a higher TouchPoints tier earns more per dirham
on the same volume.
