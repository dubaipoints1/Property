The ADCB TouchPoints Titanium Gold is the entry tier of ADCB's ADCB TouchPoints
Visa line — free for life, AED 5,000 minimum salary, no salary transfer
required. It earns ADCB TouchPoints on every dirham of spend — at reduced
rates on supermarket, fuel and government categories — with a monthly
bonus-point mechanic that scales on spend volume.

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="adcb-touchpoints-titanium-gold" />

The card is free for life on the published schedule — no annual fee, no joining fee.
The FX fee is **2.99%** per the ADCB Schedule of Fees Ver.46 (February 2026)
— in line with the typical UAE 2.49–3.5% band, no FX advantage on overseas
spend. Minimum salary is **AED 5,000 a month** and salary transfer is not
required.

## What you get — per-benefit value

The Titanium Gold's headline currency is ADCB TouchPoints, and ADCB
publishes neither a TouchPoints→AED redemption rate nor a Skywards
exchange ratio — so, as on the [ADCB Betaqti](/cards/adcb-betaqti/),
the biggest line item carries no AED figure by design, not by
oversight. What can be counted, we count against an annual fee of
AED 0.

### TouchPoints earn + the 5,000-point monthly bonus

Base earn is 0.5 TouchPoints per AED 1 (0.4 on supermarkets, insurance
and quick-service; 0.2 on fuel, education, government and telecom). On
a qualifying AED 5,000 month, the 5,000-point bonus lifts the month's
total from 2,500 to 7,500 points — an effective 1.5 per AED 1, triple
the base rate.
**Value to me: not computable — conversion unpublished.** Without a
published TouchPoints→AED rate or Skywards ratio, any dirham figure
here would be a guess. Redeem in-store and note what the till takes
off — that's the only honest valuation available.

### VOX Cinemas BOGO (up to 2 free tickets a month)

Buy one, get one free on 2D Standard and 2D Max Standard formats, up
to 2 complimentary tickets per month. At the AED 40–50 typical UAE
ticket: one paired visit a month is **Value to me: AED 480–600/year**;
the full two-free-tickets ration is **AED 960–1,200/year**, which few
readers will sustain.

### Talabat 20% off, once a month

Up to 20% off one grocery or food order per month via the MASTERCARD
promo code, capped at AED 14 per order.
**Value to me: AED 168/year** at full utilisation (12 × AED 14); AED 0
if Talabat isn't already in your routine.

**Total package:** the countable items — cinema plus Talabat — sum to
roughly **AED 650–1,370/year** depending on utilisation, against no
annual fee. Everything above that rides on the TouchPoints flows,
which ADCB leaves unpriced. On a free card that is an acceptable
unknown — but verify a redemption rate that works for your spend
before treating the points as income.

## Watch out for

- **The AED 5,000 spend gate.** The monthly bonus does the heavy lifting on
  this card. Drop below AED 5,000 in any statement month and the bonus
  doesn't pay — and the effective earn rate falls from 1.5 to the bare
  0.5-per-AED base.
- **No lounge or travel perks** on the issuer free-text. This is a domestic
  ADCB TouchPoints earner, not a travel card. Don't apply expecting Visa
  Airport Companion access without verifying it on the current product page.
- **TouchPoints exchange ratio.** ADCB TouchPoints exchange into Emirates
  Skywards Miles per the perks list, but the ratio is not published — the value
  of the airline route is uncomputable until the conversion rate is verified.
- **Step-up at sustained AED 10,000+ monthly spend.** The Platinum and
  Betaqti tiers earn more per dirham and unlock anniversary bonus mechanics
  the Titanium Gold does not. At five-figure monthly spend, the step up
  pays back fast.

## Is it worth it?

For a reader at the AED 5,000–10,000 salary band who wants a free-for-life
ADCB TouchPoints earner, will reliably clear the AED 5,000 monthly spend
gate, and values in-store redemption at the LuLu/Carrefour/ADNOC partner
network over an airline programme: the TouchPoints Titanium Gold is the right
entry. The 2.99% FX fee is market-standard for UAE Visas — no advantage on
overseas spend, but no penalty either; take a dedicated travel card abroad
if international spend is material.

For a reader running household spend through one card and clearing AED 10,000
or more per month, the step up to a Platinum or Betaqti TouchPoints tier
earns more per dirham on the same volume — and at sustained spend, the
anniversary bonus mechanic on those higher tiers more than covers the AED 0
to AED 2,100 fee gap. Match the tier to the spend pattern, not the marketing.

## Bottom line

A free-for-life ADCB TouchPoints earner at the AED 5,000 salary bar, with a
monthly bonus-point mechanic that triples the effective rate on qualifying
months. **Apply** if you want a simple
TouchPoints-currency entry with no annual-fee risk. **Skip** if you'll run
AED 10,000+ monthly spend — a higher TouchPoints tier earns more per dirham
on the same volume.
