The LuLu 247 Platinum is Emirates NBD's co-branded Mastercard with LuLu Hypermarkets. It's a
free-for-life card with a strong 7% earn rate at LuLu stores, making it particularly valuable
for families who regularly shop at LuLu across the UAE.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are **% back as LuLu Points** (1 LuLu Point ≈ AED 0.01 in redemption value):

| Spend category | Rate |
|---|---|
| LuLu in-store and luluhypermarket.com | **7%** |
| Fuel | **4%** |
| Everything else | **1%** |

Lower-earning categories (at ~25% of the standard non-LuLu rate per search data) include:
car dealerships, non-LuLu grocery/supermarkets, insurance, and fast-food restaurants.
Lowest-earning categories include education, government services, and real estate.

## LuLu 247 Platinum vs Titanium

| Feature | Titanium (Free) | Platinum (Free) |
|---|---|---|
| Min salary | ~AED 8,000 | AED 15,000 |
| LuLu earn rate | 3.5% | **7%** |
| Fuel earn rate | ~2% | 4% |
| Lounge access | 10+ ME lounges | 25+ global lounges |

## Notes

- Free-for-life card — no annual or joining fee to justify.
- LuLu Points only redeem within LuLu ecosystem (stores and website) — not transferable to airlines.
- Best value for families who spend AED 3,000+ per month at LuLu: 7% back = AED 210+/month.
- Lounge access covers key ME destinations plus major international hubs.
