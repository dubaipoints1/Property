The Darna Visa Signature is the mid-tier card in Emirates NBD's Darna programme.
AED 0 annual fee at an AED 12,000 minimum salary, 0% FX fee, and the same
Darna redemption mechanics as the higher-tier Infinite — applied against rent,
service charges, or developer payments at participating Dubai providers.

## Earn rates

| Spend category | Darna Points per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Everything else | **1** |

Flat earn — the redemption mechanic, not the earn rate, is the value.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 0 |
| FX fee | 0% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 12,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not required |

The fee profile is the headline: **AED 0 + 0% FX** on a Signature-tier card is
unusual in the UAE market.

## Key conditions to know

**Darna redemption network:**
- Points credit monthly to your Darna account
- Redeem against rent, service charges, or developer bills at participating
  Dubai property providers
- Confirm your landlord or building manager is in the network before applying

**Signature-tier benefits:**
- Travel insurance and purchase protection (verify cover in the issuer
  certificate)
- Visa Signature concierge support (region-dependent — confirm scope)
- Limited lounge access — confirm provider and visit cap with ENBD

## Darna Select vs Signature vs Infinite

| Dimension | [Select](/cards/emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa/) | Signature | [Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-darna-visa-infinite/) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 0 | AED 0 | AED 1,575 |
| Minimum salary | AED 5,000 | AED 12,000 | AED 30,000 |
| FX fee | 0% | 0% | 0% |
| Salary transfer | Not required | Not required | **Required** |
| Tier benefits | Basic Visa | Signature suite | Visa Infinite suite |

The Signature is the rational mid-band choice — same fee profile as the Select
but with broader Signature-tier perks. The jump to Infinite only makes sense
if Visa Infinite concierge or global lounge access is material to you.

## Best for

Dubai residents on AED 12,000+ a month who already participate in the Darna
ecosystem — typically Damac, Emaar, or DMCC tenants — and want a zero-fee
card that converts spend into housing credit. Also serviceable as a
foreign-currency shopping card given the 0% FX fee.

## Watch out for

- **Narrow redemption network.** Darna Points are useless outside the
  participating-developer list — verify your provider before applying
- **Welcome bonus terms** are not consistently documented on the issuer
  marketing material at our last verification — confirm directly with ENBD
- **1% earn rate** is below par; pair with a cashback or category card for
  groceries, fuel, and dining
- **Signature-tier benefits vary.** Concierge and lounge cover differ by
  region — read the Visa Signature certificate at activation
