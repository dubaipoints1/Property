The Emirates NBD Etihad Guest Visa Elevate is the middle rung in ENBD's Etihad
co-brand ladder, sitting between the AED 12,000-salary Inspire and the AED
30,000-salary FAB Etihad Guest Infinite from a competing issuer. It earns
Etihad Guest Miles directly into the cardholder's account at the AED 30,000
salary bar, with salary transfer required.

<HotTip>
The Elevate's **10 Etihad Guest Miles per AED 10** on Etihad airfare, hotels
and dining is the highest Etihad-ecosystem rate of the three UAE Etihad
co-brands — ahead of the **7 per AED 10** that both the ENBD Inspire and the
FAB Etihad Guest Infinite pay on Etihad spend. But read the "up to 6 Miles on
all other spends" line carefully: it is an envelope, not a rate. International
spend earns **3 per AED 10** and everything else **2** — below the Inspire's
4 and the FAB Infinite's 3.5 base, and the FAB Infinite pays 6 per AED 10 on
international. Concentrate this card on Etihad, hotels and dining; route
general and overseas spend through a stronger base earner.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-etihad-guest-elevate" />

Year one costs **AED 2,625** — the joining fee, including VAT, per the ENBD
product page and the May 2026 KFS. From renewal the fee drops to **AED 1,575**
a year. No waiver is published for either figure, so budget the higher number
before applying. The **1.99%** FX fee is below the 2.49% norm on most premium
UAE Visa Infinite cards in market — useful for an expat whose foreign-currency
spend is meaningful. Minimum salary is **AED 30,000 a month** and salary
transfer is required — a material differentiator from both the ENBD Inspire
(no transfer required, AED 12,000 salary bar) and the FAB Etihad Guest
Infinite (no transfer required at the same salary bar).

## What you get — per-benefit value

Per the [2 fils-per-mile baseline](/airlines/etihad-guest/) we use for
every UAE Etihad Guest co-brand review — the same framework used for
Skywards, applied symmetrically across the two UAE flag carriers. Add
the numbers up against the AED 2,625 year-one joining fee and the
AED 1,575 renewal fee thereafter.

### Welcome bonus

Up to 200,000 Etihad Guest Miles on activation and qualifying spend —
the largest welcome figure on any UAE-issued Etihad co-brand at our
11 June 2026 verification (the FAB Infinite's limited-time offer runs
to 110,000; the Inspire's to 60,000). **Value to me: AED 4,000** at the
2-fil baseline — which clears the AED 2,625 year-one joining fee on the
welcome alone, with AED 1,375 of headroom before counting any earn or
lounge value. Burnt on a long-haul Business saver one-way (AUH → JFK
from ~88,000 Miles per the Etihad Guest sweet-spot list), the realised
cash-fare value typically lands AED 8,000–14,000 against the same miles
— but the cost-basis figure is the honest welcome-bonus framing for
break-even math before applying.

### Visa Airport Companion lounge access

Unlimited cardholder + 1 guest at 1,000+ lounges worldwide. At an
AED 200 walk-in equivalent per visit: **Value to me: AED 2,400/year**
for a regular flyer (six trips with a guest); **AED 800/year** for a
two-trip-a-year traveller. Independent of Etihad Guest tier — useful
on a non-Etihad sector where Gold lounge access wouldn't apply.

### 50% redemption-cost voucher on AED 150,000 spend

A 50% discount voucher applied to a redemption-cost basis. Against a
long-haul Business saver redemption (typically 88,000–120,000 Miles
one-way), the voucher saves 44,000–60,000 Miles — at the 2-fil
baseline, **Value to me: AED 880–1,200/year** if you clear AED 150,000
of spend and actually redeem for a Business sector. Worth zero if you
don't clear the threshold or don't redeem.

### Earn on AED 100,000 of card spend

At 10 Etihad Guest Miles per AED 10 on Etihad airfare, hotels and
dining (1 Mile per AED 1 — about 2 fils of value per dirham at our
baseline) and 2 per AED 10 on general spend (0.2 Miles per AED 1 —
about 0.4 fils per dirham), a typical AED 100,000 mixed spend year
(assume AED 25,000 Etihad/hotels/dining, AED 75,000 general) returns
~40,000 Miles. At the 2-fil baseline: **Value to me: AED 800/year**.
The Etihad-ecosystem rate is the best of the three UAE Etihad
co-brands, but the 0.2-Miles-per-AED base drags the blended return —
the more your spend skews general, the more the Inspire's 4 per AED 10
base out-earns it.

### Year-one Etihad Guest tier status

No automatic tier upgrade comes with this card — Etihad Guest tier
status from the Elevate is **Value to me: AED 0/year**. Honest line:
if tier is what you want, the FAB Etihad Guest Infinite's Gold-tier
fast-track on one return flight is the relevant lever, not this card.

For an AED 30,000-salary holder who routes salary to ENBD, runs
AED 100,000 a year through the card, and burns the 200,000-mile welcome
on a long-haul Business one-way in year one, the total package delivers
roughly **AED 7,200–8,400** of value in year one (AED 4,000 welcome +
AED 800 earn + AED 2,400 lounge for a regular flyer, plus the voucher
only if you clear AED 150,000) against the **AED 2,625** joining fee —
the welcome alone covers the fee with AED 1,375 to spare. Steady state,
the same profile returns roughly **AED 1,600–3,200/year** (earn plus
lounge, voucher excluded) against **AED 1,575** of renewal fee —
comfortably positive for a regular flyer, marginal for a
two-trip-a-year traveller.

## Watch out for

- **AED 2,625 joining fee in year one.** The widely quoted AED 1,575 is the
  renewal fee, not the cost of getting in — the product page and the May 2026
  KFS both carry the AED 2,625 joining fee, and no waiver is published for
  either figure.
- **Salary transfer is mandatory.** Unlike the Inspire and the FAB Infinite,
  the Elevate requires committing your salary to ENBD — a banking decision
  that affects your salary-transfer-incentive eligibility elsewhere. Check the
  [salary-transfer tracker](/salary-transfer/) before applying.
- **General spend earns less than the headline.** The "up to 6 Miles per
  AED 10 on all other spends" line resolves to 3 per AED 10 on international
  and 2 on everything else — below the Inspire's 4 and the FAB Infinite's 3.5
  base, and the FAB Infinite pays 6 per AED 10 on international spend.
- **2 miles per AED 10 on general spend** is roughly 0.2 miles per AED 1 — a
  flat cashback card pays back more on UAE in-store spend.
- **Tier Mile cap and from-flying floors.** The 2.5 Tier Miles per AED 10
  rate is uncapped within the card's terms but still cannot fulfil the
  from-flying minimums each Etihad Guest tier requires — you cannot card-spend
  your way to status.
- **Etihad-loyal only.** A Dubai-based Skywards flyer earns more per dirham
  on an [ENBD or FAB Skywards co-brand](/airlines/skywards/).

## Bottom line

The Elevate's claim to this market is now twofold: the 200,000-mile welcome
and the strongest Etihad-ecosystem earn of any UAE Etihad co-brand — 10
Etihad Guest Miles per AED 10 on Etihad airfare, hotels and dining. For an
Abu Dhabi or northern-emirates resident who flies Etihad two or three times a
year, spends in those categories, and is willing to transfer salary to ENBD,
the welcome alone — AED 4,000 at the 2-fil baseline — clears the AED 2,625
joining fee with AED 1,375 to spare and funds a long-haul Business one-way at
saver rate with headroom remaining.

If you'd rather keep your salary elsewhere, the
[FAB Etihad Guest Infinite](/cards/fab-etihad-guest-infinite/) earns 7 Miles
per AED 10 on Etihad spend — below the Elevate's 10 — but 6 per AED 10 on
international, with no transfer requirement, an AED 2,625 fee every year
(the Elevate drops to AED 1,575 at renewal) and a 110,000-Mile welcome
running to 30 June 2026. At AED 12k–25k salary, the
[ENBD Inspire](/cards/emirates-nbd-etihad-guest-inspire/) earns 7 per AED 10
on Etihad and a stronger 4 per AED 10 on everything else at an AED 735
renewal fee. Spend concentrated on Etihad, hotels and dining with ENBD
banking in place: take the Elevate. Spend that's mostly general or
international: take a sibling.
