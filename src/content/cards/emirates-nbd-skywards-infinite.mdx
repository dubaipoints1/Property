The Skywards Infinite is Emirates NBD's flagship co-branded card with Emirates Skywards.
Geared at frequent Emirates flyers based in the UAE.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are in **Skywards Miles per AED 1** of eligible spend:

| Category | Miles per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Dining & travel | **2.5** |
| Everything else | **1** |

## Notes

- Lounge access is via DragonPass, not Priority Pass.
- Salary transfer not strictly required but improves approval odds.
- Annual fee waiver requires AED 100,000 annual spend from year two.
