The Skywards Infinite is Emirates NBD's flagship co-branded Visa Infinite card with
Emirates Skywards. Built for frequent Emirates flyers based in the UAE: 2 Skywards
Miles per USD on Emirates, flydubai, duty free, and online food delivery and ride-hailing
apps; direct Skywards earning with no transfer step; and an AED 1,575 year-two fee
that's only defensible if you'll genuinely use the miles.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are credited as **Skywards Miles per USD 1** of eligible spend:

| Spend category | Miles per USD 1 |
|---|---|
| Emirates, flydubai, duty free, food delivery, ride-hailing apps | **2** |
| International spend (non-AED) | **1.5** |
| Domestic UAE spend | **1** |
| EU and UK spend | **0.5** |

The bank denominates earn in USD across the entire Skywards-co-brand range. At a working
rate of USD 1 ≈ AED 3.67, 2 miles per USD on partner categories is roughly 0.54 miles
per AED 1 (or 5.4 miles per AED 10) — useful for back-of-envelope, but the bank's earn
statements always use the USD figure.

Miles post directly to your Emirates Skywards account — no monthly conversion step and
no transfer ratio loss.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (year one) | AED 3,148.95 |
| Annual fee, year one | AED 0 (waived for first-year applicants) |
| Annual fee, year two onwards | AED 1,575 |
| Year-two waiver threshold | AED 100,000 annual spend |
| Interest rate | 3.25% per month |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 30,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not strictly required (improves approval odds) |

The year-two waiver is reversible — fall below AED 100,000 of annual spend and the
fee returns the following year. Plan for AED 1,575 unless your card is genuinely
your primary spend vehicle.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

- **Up to 100,000 bonus Skywards Miles** — the headline offer on activation
- **Complimentary 1-year Rotana Rewards Exclusive membership** — bundled with activation
- **Automatic Emirates Skywards Silver tier upgrade** — for the bonus qualification window
- Headline value: ~AED 2,000 at our 2 fils-per-mile baseline (see our
  [Skywards valuation](/airlines/skywards/) — we use 2 fils as the conservative
  redemption floor; one well-priced Y-to-J upgrade on a regional sector lifts the
  effective rate higher)

The bonus alone covers the year-two fee with substantial room to spare, provided
you upgrade rather than redeem at the Skywards economy chart.

## Key conditions to know

**Lounge access:**
- **Visa Airport Companion App**, not Priority Pass. Unlimited complimentary visits
  for the cardholder plus one guest at 1,000+ lounges worldwide
- Includes DXB, AUH, and major hub airports

**Skywards status match:**
- **Skywards Silver** tier upgrade for the welcome window
- Silver buys priority check-in, extra baggage, and tier miles toward Gold
- Lost from year two unless you re-qualify on flown tier miles

**Express Miles add-on:**
- Optional AED 250 + 5% VAT per month
- Earns 50% more miles on all non-Emirates purchases
- Replaces the annual fee in the months it's active

**Salary transfer:**
- Not strictly required, but improves approval odds at AED 30,000/month
- ENBD typically asks for 3 months of payslips and a stamped salary
  certificate — the [salary-transfer tracker](/salary-transfer/) covers the
  current waiver mechanics

**Annual fee waiver mechanics:**
- AED 100,000 spend is **cumulative annual**, not per statement
- Resets each card anniversary
- ENBD applies the waiver retroactively — the fee posts and is reversed,
  rather than being skipped at the threshold

## Skywards Infinite vs Skywards Signature

| Dimension | Infinite | [Signature](/cards/emirates-nbd-skywards-signature/) |
|---|---|---|
| Annual fee (year 2+) | AED 1,575 | AED 735 |
| Joining fee (year 1) | AED 3,148.95 | AED 1,573.95 |
| Minimum salary | AED 30,000 | AED 12,000 |
| Top earn rate | 2 miles / USD (partner) | 1.5 miles / USD (partner) |
| Lounge access | Visa Airport Companion (1,000+ lounges, + 1 guest) | Visa Airport Companion (cardholder only) |
| Welcome bonus | Up to 100,000 miles + 1-year Rotana Rewards | Up to 40,000 miles |

The Infinite makes the most sense if both conditions hold: you'll trigger the
year-one bonus quickly **and** you'll spend AED 100,000+ a year on the card
ongoing. Otherwise the Signature carries lower fee risk for a similar earn
profile.

## Best for

UAE-based Emirates flyers who already book two or more long-haul sectors a year
out of DXB, want direct Skywards earning, and run AED 100,000+ of card spend a
year. The card pays for itself most clearly when the welcome bonus funds a
single Y-to-J upgrade — that one redemption alone typically exceeds the
two-year fee burden.

## Watch out for

- **Devaluation risk.** Skywards has repriced the partner award chart twice
  in five years. A 2 miles per USD earn on partner categories is only valuable
  if the underlying miles hold their value — keep a redemption goal close at hand
- **Lounge provider.** Visa Airport Companion coverage is strong in the Gulf, Asia,
  and most of Europe; double-check less-trafficked regional airports before you travel
- **Status match expires.** Silver in year one is generous; you lose it in
  year two unless your flown tier miles re-qualify you
- **Spend-waiver cliff.** Slipping under AED 100,000 in any year reinstates the
  AED 1,575 charge — model your year-two and year-three spend before applying
- **Joining-fee hit.** AED 3,148.95 in year one is recovered only if you
  trigger the full welcome bonus and use the miles. If either fails, the
  Signature is the cheaper entry point
- **Better alternatives at lower fees.** If you fly Etihad more than Emirates,
  the [Etihad Guest Inspire](/cards/emirates-nbd-etihad-guest-inspire/) or
  the [FAB Etihad Guest Infinite](/cards/fab-etihad-guest-infinite/) carry
  lower fees and similar earn rates on the Abu Dhabi carrier
