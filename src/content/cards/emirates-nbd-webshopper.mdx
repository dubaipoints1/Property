The Emirates NBD WebShopper is a purpose-built online-shopping card — AED 52.50
annual fee, 1.99% FX fee, and a roughly 1% Plus Points earn rate. Designed to be
used exclusively for e-commerce, separate from your in-store card.

The fee profile is modest: AED 52.50 annual cost,
1.99% FX, AED 5,000 minimum salary — the low salary bar is its main draw.

## Key conditions to know

**Intended use — online only:**
- Marketed for e-commerce; physical-merchant transactions earn the same
  flat 1% but you'd typically use a category card in-store
- Card number is the standard 16-digit format — accepted by any online
  merchant that accepts Visa
- Some users keep the card unactivated for physical use to reduce
  card-cloning risk

**FX fee:**
- 1.99% FX, in line with the UAE market norm of 1.99–2.49% — no saving on
  international e-commerce
- On AED 5,000 of annual non-AED online spend, the FX cost is
  ~AED 100 — the standard surcharge

## Best for

UAE residents on AED 5,000+ a month who shop online internationally
(Amazon, AliExpress, ASOS, Shein, Etsy) and want a dedicated card for
that traffic. Particularly useful for new residents who don't yet
qualify for higher-tier cards but want zero FX on their first online
purchases.

## Watch out for

- **1% earn is the floor.** The card pays a thin reward — and with a
  1.99% FX fee there is no FX-fee saving to lean on either
- **Single-purpose.** Off-online spending earns the same 1% with no
  category bonuses — pair with a category card for groceries and dining
- **Welcome bonus not documented** at our last verification
- **Online-fraud exposure.** Keeping a dedicated card for e-commerce
  reduces the blast radius if a merchant is breached, but only works if
  you actually leave the physical card at home — not just symbolically
