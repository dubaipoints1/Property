The Priority Banking Visa Infinite is Emirates NBD's relationship card — issued to
existing Priority Banking customers as part of the wealth-management package. AED
1,500 annual fee, 0% FX fee, Visa Infinite benefit suite, and a flat 1% Plus
Points earn rate.

## Earn rates

<EarnRateTable card="emirates-nbd-priority-banking-visa-infinite" />

Earn is flat. The card's value sits in the Visa Infinite perks and the wider
Priority Banking relationship, not in points generation on day-to-day spend.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 1,500 |
| FX fee | 0% |
| Minimum monthly salary | Priority Banking qualification required |
| Salary transfer | **Required** |

The Priority Banking relationship typically requires AED 350,000+ in total assets
under management with the bank, **or** a salary of AED 50,000+ per month
credited to ENBD. Confirm current thresholds with your relationship manager
before applying.

## Key conditions to know

**Priority Banking relationship:**
- Card is **only issued to existing Priority Banking customers** — you cannot
  apply for the card without the underlying relationship
- Priority Banking benefits typically include a dedicated relationship
  manager, preferential FX rates, fee waivers on transfers, and access to
  Priority Banking lounges in DXB and select ENBD branches
- Card fee is sometimes waived for Premier-tier Priority customers — confirm
  with your RM

**Visa Infinite benefits:**
- Visa Infinite concierge service
- Global lounge access (verify provider and visit cap — historically
  DragonPass-based)
- Visa Infinite travel insurance, purchase protection, extended warranty
- Travel and lifestyle privileges through Visa's regional partner network

**Salary-transfer mechanics:**
- For salaried applicants, mandatory transfer to ENBD
- For business owners and self-employed, the AUM threshold path applies
  instead

## Best for

Existing Emirates NBD Priority Banking customers who want a Visa Infinite
that aligns with their wealth-management relationship and who value the 0% FX
fee for international spend. The card is not a rational standalone choice —
it's a relationship product.

## Watch out for

- **Not openly available.** If you're not already a Priority Banking
  customer, applying for this card means opening the entire wealth-management
  relationship first
- **AED 1,500 fee for 1% earn** is poor on standalone economics — the
  relationship perks have to do the heavy lifting
- **Welcome bonus terms** not consistently documented at our last
  verification — confirm with your RM
- **Fee waiver is discretionary.** Premier-tier customers sometimes have
  the AED 1,500 waived; verify the current policy applies to your tier
- **Compare to relationship alternatives.** Mashreq Gold, ADCB Privilege,
  and FAB Premier all offer comparable wealth-relationship cards — the
  decision usually follows your primary banking, not the other way around
