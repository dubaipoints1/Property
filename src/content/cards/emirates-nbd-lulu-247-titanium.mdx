The LuLu 247 Titanium is Emirates NBD's entry-level LuLu co-branded Mastercard. Free for life with
a lower income threshold than the Platinum, it's a starting point for LuLu shoppers on more modest
incomes who want points on everyday grocery spending.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are **% back as LuLu Points** (1 LuLu Point ≈ AED 0.01 in redemption value):

| Spend category | Titanium | Platinum |
|---|---|---|
| LuLu in-store / online | **3.5%** | 7% |
| Fuel | **2%** | 4% |
| Everything else | **0.5%** | 1% |

## Notes

- Free-for-life with no income-justification needed — any UAE resident on AED 8,000/month qualifies.
- Lounge access limited to 10+ ME airports; Platinum covers 25+ global destinations.
- Upgrade path: once salary exceeds AED 15,000, step up to Platinum to double all earn rates.
- LuLu Points only redeem within LuLu ecosystem — not transferable to airlines or hotels.
