The LuLu 247 Titanium is Emirates NBD's entry-level LuLu co-branded Mastercard.
Free for life, with a lower income threshold than the Platinum, it's a starting
point for LuLu shoppers on more modest incomes who want points on everyday
grocery spending.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are **% back as LuLu Points** (1 LuLu Point ≈ AED 0.01 in
redemption value):

| Spend category | Titanium | [Platinum](/cards/emirates-nbd-lulu-247-platinum/) |
|---|---|---|
| LuLu in-store / online | **3.5%** | 7% |
| Fuel | **2%** | 4% |
| Everything else | **0.5%** | 1% |

LuLu Points redeem at the till at LuLu hypermarkets and online — no transfer
to airlines, hotels, or third-party programmes.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 0 (free for life) |
| Interest rate | 3.49% per month |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 8,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not required |

The fee structure is the simplest in the ENBD lineup — no fee, no waiver
threshold, no clawback. The cost sits entirely in the interest rate if you
revolve a balance.

## Key conditions to know

**Lounge access:**
- Complimentary access to **10+ airport lounges across the Middle East** —
  DragonPass-style network
- Coverage is regional, not global; the Platinum extends to 25+ destinations
  worldwide

**Travel desk and golf:**
- 3% discount on flight bookings via ENBD Travel Desk
- 7% off holiday packages booked through the same channel
- 40% off green fees at 100+ golf courses globally (limited rounds per year —
  verify current programme)

**LuLu redemption mechanics:**
- Points credited monthly to your LuLu loyalty account
- Redeem at the till or online — no minimum balance
- Points **expire after 12 months** of inactivity

## Best for

Lower-income UAE residents who shop at LuLu weekly. The card pays for itself
on grocery spend alone: a household putting AED 3,000 a month through LuLu
earns AED 105 a month in points (AED 1,260 a year). At AED 0 in fees, every
dirham of earn is net value.

## Watch out for

- **Opportunity cost vs Platinum.** Earn half the points the Platinum does
  across every category. If your salary clears AED 15,000, the Platinum's
  AED 250 annual fee is recouped in roughly four months of typical LuLu spend
- **Narrow redemption.** LuLu Points only work at LuLu — if you wouldn't
  shop there otherwise, the points have zero realisable value
- **Weak base earn.** 0.5% on non-grocery, non-fuel spend is the lowest base
  rate in the ENBD card stack; pair this with a flat-rate cashback card for
  everything that isn't LuLu or fuel
- **Lounge coverage is regional.** Don't apply for the lounge access alone —
  ten-plus Middle East lounges is useful for short-haul, less so if you're
  flying long-haul out of DXB and want a Priority Pass-style global network
