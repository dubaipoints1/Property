The U by Emaar Infinite is Emirates NBD's co-branded Visa Infinite card with Emaar's
loyalty programme. It is purpose-built for UAE residents who spend heavily at Emaar
properties — Dubai Mall, Address Hotels, Rove, Vida, and Emaar's F&B outlets.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are expressed as **UPoints percentage back** (not miles per AED):

| Spend category | Rate |
|---|---|
| All Emaar spends (shops, hotels, dining) | **7.5%** back |
| Everything else | **1.5%** back |

UPoints are credited to your U by Emaar account, not your bank account.

## Fees

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (one-time) | AED 2,625 |
| Annual renewal fee | AED 1,575 |
| Interest rate | 3.25% per month |
| Minimum payment | 5% of outstanding balance or AED 100, whichever is higher |
| FX fee | 1.99% (verify against KFS) |

## Welcome bonus

- **15,000 UPoints** credited on card activation + payment of joining fee
- **10,000 additional UPoints** on cumulative retail purchases of USD 10,000 within the first three billing statements
- **Total: up to 25,000 UPoints**
- Automatic upgrade to **U by Emaar Platinum status** upon card activation

## Reel Cinemas — key conditions

- Minimum **AED 5,000 monthly spend** required to unlock Buy 1 Get 1 free tickets
- Maximum **3 free tickets (6 total) per month**; additional tickets charged at full price
- Must book **online** via Reel Cinemas website or app
- Includes complimentary soft drink and popcorn

## Hotel upgrade — key conditions

- Complimentary suite upgrade capped at **7 nights per annum**
- Late checkout until 4pm subject to availability
- Member rates apply for bookings via brand websites or U by Emaar app

## Valet parking note

From 1 May 2025, Grand Drive valet at Dubai Mall is unavailable due to expansion.
U by Emaar Infinite cardholders are redirected to the **China Town valet area**.
From 25 January 2025, valet access requires generating an OTP in the U by Emaar app.

## Best for

Residents who shop, eat, or stay at Emaar properties regularly — particularly Dubai Mall
shoppers, Address/Rove/Vida hotel guests, and Reel Cinemas regulars. The 7.5% back on
all Emaar spend is among the highest category earn rates on any UAE card.

## Watch out for

- The **AED 2,625 joining fee** is steep; factor this into year-one value calculations
- **1.5% back** on non-Emaar spend is competitive but not best-in-class
- UPoints are only redeemable within the Emaar ecosystem — not transferable to airlines
- Reel Cinemas BOGO requires AED 5,000/month minimum spend to unlock
