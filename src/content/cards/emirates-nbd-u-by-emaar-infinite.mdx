The U by Emaar Infinite is Emirates NBD's co-branded Visa Infinite card with the U by Emaar
loyalty programme. Built for residents who spend heavily at Emaar properties — Dubai Mall,
Address/Rove/Vida hotels, and Emaar's F&B outlets.

## Earn rates

All earn rates are expressed as **% back in UPoints** (not miles per AED):

| Spend category | Rate |
|---|---|
| All Emaar spends (malls, hotels, dining) | **7.5%** |
| Everything else | **1.5%** |

UPoints are credited to your U by Emaar account. They are **not transferable to airlines**.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (one-time, year 1) | AED 2,625 |
| Annual renewal fee (year 2+) | AED 1,575 |
| Interest rate | 3.25% per month |
| Minimum payment | 5% of outstanding or AED 100 (higher applies) |
| FX fee | ~1.99% (verify against KFS) |

## Welcome bonus breakdown

- **15,000 UPoints** — credited automatically on card activation + joining fee payment
- **10,000 UPoints** — on cumulative retail spend of USD 10,000 within first 3 billing statements
- **Automatic U by Emaar Platinum status** on activation

## Key conditions to know

**Reel Cinemas BOGO:**
- Requires AED 5,000 minimum monthly card spend
- Max 3 free tickets (6 total) per calendar month
- Must book online via Reel Cinemas website or app

**Hotel suite upgrade:**
- Capped at 7 complimentary nights per year
- Late checkout until 4pm, subject to availability
- Book via brand websites or U by Emaar app for member rates

**Valet parking (as of May 2025):**
- Grand Drive valet at Dubai Mall unavailable due to expansion — redirected to China Town valet area
- Access via OTP generated in the U by Emaar app (required from Jan 2025)

## Best for

Residents who regularly shop, eat, or stay within the Emaar ecosystem. The 7.5% back on
all Emaar spend is among the strongest category earn rates on any UAE card. Particularly
strong for Dubai Mall regulars, Reel Cinemas users, and Address/Rove/Vida hotel guests.

## Watch out for

- AED 2,625 joining fee is steep — calculate your year-one net value carefully
- UPoints **only redeem within the Emaar ecosystem** — no airline or hotel transfers
- 1.5% on non-Emaar spend is decent but not market-leading for a card at this fee level
- Reel Cinemas BOGO requires AED 5k/month minimum — check your typical spend first
