The Emirates NBD U by Emaar Infinite is the bank's co-branded Visa Infinite with the U by
Emaar loyalty programme. It is built for the resident whose discretionary spend genuinely
runs through Emaar's footprint — Dubai Mall, the Address/Vida/Rove hotel families, and
Emaar F&B — and earns 7.5% back as UPoints across that universe.

<HotTip>
On a 7.5% headline, the AED 2,625 joining fee breaks even at roughly **AED 35,000 a
year** — about AED 2,900 a month — of qualifying Emaar spend (AED 2,625 ÷ 7.5%). From
year two the AED 1,575 renewal needs ~AED 21,000 a year (about AED 1,750 a month). Run
your own twelve-month Emaar total before applying — anything below that line, and a
no-fee ENBD Plus Points card plus a separate U by Emaar account earns the loyalty side
without the card fees.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-u-by-emaar-infinite" />

The card carries an **AED 2,625 joining fee** in year one, on top of the **AED 1,575
annual renewal** from year two onwards — both inclusive of VAT. There is **no published
spend-based waiver** on either fee. The **foreign-currency fee is 1.99%**, levied on every
non-AED transaction. As with any rewards card, clear the statement in full each month —
the 3.25% monthly interest charge will erase any UPoints value many times over.

## Perks worth using

The U by Emaar Infinite is a high-fee Visa Infinite — AED 2,625 joining + AED
1,575 renewal — so the perk stack has to do real work alongside the 7.5% UPoints
rate. We've put a per-benefit Value to me on each so you can see whether the
chrome clears its share of the AED 2,625 year-one joining fee (the AED 1,575
renewal applies from year two):

### 7.5% UPoints on Emaar spend

The headline rate. Household routing AED 8,000/month through Dubai Mall,
Address F&B and Emaar retail: 8,000 × 12 × 7.5% = **Value to me:
AED 7,200/year** in UPoints. Heavy Emaar use (AED 20,000/month — the audience
this card is built for): **Value to me: AED 18,000/year**. Light Emaar use
(AED 2,500/month): **AED 2,250/year** — clears the AED 1,575 renewal but falls
short of year one's AED 2,625 joining fee. The year-one break-even sits at
~AED 2,900/month of Emaar spend, as the Hot tip flagged.

### Automatic U by Emaar Platinum status

Tier benefits include 20% off Emaar Hospitality F&B (Address, Vida, Rove
restaurants) and 20% off spa treatments. A household dining Emaar F&B
AED 1,500/month at 20% off = AED 300/month saved = **Value to me:
AED 3,600/year**. Spa users add roughly **AED 400–800/year**. Status alone (no
discount) wouldn't earn this back; the discount is where the AED lands.

### Suite upgrades (max 7 nights/year) at Address/Vida/Rove

Suite-vs-standard delta at an Address property typically AED 600–1,200/night
depending on season and property. Conservative AED 600 × 7 = **Value to me:
AED 4,200/year** if you'll actually book and use the 7-night ration. Most
holders use 2–3 nights/year — realistic mid case **AED 1,200–1,800/year**.

### Unlimited Visa Airport Companion lounge access

The Infinite-tier unlimited-lounges benefit is the genuine chrome here.
1,000+ lounges globally at an AED 200 walk-in equivalent. Four trips a year
with a partner (8 entries) = **Value to me: AED 1,600/year**. Heavy traveller
on 10 trips with guest: **AED 4,000/year**. Most UAE residents fly 4–6 trips
a year — the realistic value lands at **AED 1,600–2,400/year**.

### Reel Cinemas BOGO (AED 5,000 monthly spend gate)

Buy-one-get-one at Reel with a free soft drink + popcorn, capped at 3 free
tickets per calendar month. Reel standard pricing roughly AED 55/ticket plus
AED 25 F&B. Three paired visits/month = 3 × (AED 55 + AED 25) × 12 =
**Value to me: AED 2,880/year** at the full cap. Mid case (one paired visit
a month): **AED 960/year**. Only counts in months you clear AED 5,000 of
card spend — most holders of this card clear that.

### Welcome bonus: up to 25,000 UPoints

15,000 UPoints credited on activation and joining-fee payment, plus 10,000
UPoints on USD 10,000 (~AED 36,725) of retail spend within the first three
billing statements. UPoints redeem at par at Emaar partner outlets:
**Value to me: AED 250 on activation + AED 167 on the spend tier =
AED 417 in year one** at the standard 1 UPoint = AED 0.0167 redemption
benchmark. Confirm the current redemption rate before relying on this — the
UPoints value scale moves with Emaar's programme.

### Visa Infinite chrome (concierge, valet, EPP)

24/7 concierge and Grand Drive valet at Dubai Mall (Galeries Lafayette and
The Souk). Valet at Dubai Mall retails AED 50–80 per visit; weekly Dubai Mall
visitor (50/year × AED 65): **Value to me: AED 3,250/year** if you'd pay for
valet anyway. Light user (once a month): **AED 780/year**. 0% Easy Payment
Plans at Apple, Carrefour, Emirates, Amazon and Fitness First have an opportunity
value of roughly **AED 200–500/year** on a single AED 5,000–10,000 deferred
purchase.

For the intended audience — a Dubai Mall regular routing AED 20,000+/month
through the Emaar ecosystem, flying 4–6 trips a year, dining Emaar F&B
monthly — the perk stack delivers roughly **AED 25,000–32,000/year** in
combined UPoints + perk value against the AED 2,625 year-one joining fee
and AED 1,575 renewal. Outside that profile (Emaar spend under AED 5,000/month, no
mall valet need, no Reel cinema habit), the realistic stack collapses to
**AED 4,000–6,000/year** — a much narrower margin against the AED 2,625
joining fee.

## Watch out for

- **~AED 2,900/month of Emaar spend is the year-one break-even.** Below that, the
  AED 2,625 joining fee is not earned back on the 7.5% rate in year one. Run a twelve-month Emaar audit
  before applying — restaurants, mall purchases, hotel stays, cinema tickets — and confirm
  the number stands up.
- **UPoints lock you into the Emaar ecosystem.** They redeem at Emaar's 2,500+ partner
  outlets and nowhere else — no airline transfers, no hotel-programme conversion. If your
  travel ambitions sit outside Emaar's footprint, a Skywards or hotel-co-brand card will
  carry more redemption optionality.
- **The 1.5% on non-Emaar spend is thin.** It is only marginally above the ENBD Plus
  Points base; the SC Platinum X or Mashreq Cashback earns more on the same non-Emaar
  spend. Treat this as a single-purpose Emaar-spend card, not an everyday earner.
- **The Reel Cinemas BOGO gate.** The benefit is conditional on AED 5,000 minimum card
  spend in the month. Check your typical monthly card spend before relying on it.
- **Valet redirection at Dubai Mall.** Grand Drive valet has been periodically rerouted
  during Dubai Mall expansion works; access is via an OTP generated in the U by Emaar app.
  Re-verify the current valet location before relying on it for a planned visit.

## Bottom line

A bullseye for Dubai Mall locals and Emaar-hotel loyalists; an overpaid Visa Infinite for
everyone else. For the household genuinely spending AED 3,000+ a month inside the Emaar
footprint, the 7.5% UPoints rate is unmatched in the UAE market and the AED 2,625
year-one joining fee is recoverable — and the automatic U by Emaar Platinum status, suite
upgrades and Visa Infinite chrome round it out. **Apply** if your year of Emaar spend
clears AED 35,000 and you value the Platinum status. **Skip** if most of your spend lands
outside the Emaar footprint — the 1.5% non-Emaar rate sits only marginally above the ENBD
Plus Points base, and a no-fee ENBD Visa Flexi plus a free-standing U by Emaar account
captures the loyalty side without the card fees.
