The Go4it Gold is Emirates NBD's entry-level lifestyle card, combining Nol card
functionality with a low AED 104 annual fee. Built for UAE residents who want
RTA convenience and basic rewards without a high income threshold.

## Earn rates

| Spend type | Plus Points per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Weekend entertainment | **0.0125** (5 PP per AED 400) |
| Standard retail | **0.01** (1 PP per AED 100) |

Plus Points convert to Skywards Miles, cashback, or merchant credit at
ENBD's published conversion rates — typically realising around 1¢ per
point at the cashback ceiling.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 104 (inclusive of VAT) |
| Interest rate | 3.49% per month |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 5,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not required |

No published spend-based waiver — the AED 104 fee posts annually regardless of
card usage.

## Key conditions to know

**Nol card integration:**
- The card chip works as a **Nol card** for Dubai Metro, buses, RTA parking
  meters, and water bus
- Top up via the ENBD app, RTA top-up machines, or NFC contactless
- One complimentary Dubai Ferry ride per month for cardholder + one guest

**Life insurance:**
- AED 75,000 automatic life insurance cover for the primary cardholder
- No medical questionnaire, no application — applies on card activation
- Terms typically void on death from undeclared pre-existing conditions; read
  the issuer's certificate

**Easy Payment Plan:**
- 0% interest installment plans at 3, 6, 12, or 24 months
- Available at participating merchants in the UAE
- 1% processing fee typically applies — verify per transaction

## Go4it Gold vs Go4it Platinum

| Dimension | Gold | [Platinum](/cards/emirates-nbd-go4it-platinum/) |
|---|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 104 | AED 525 |
| Minimum salary | AED 5,000 | AED 12,000 |
| Nol integration | Yes | Yes |
| Metro Gold Class access | No | Yes |
| VOX Cinemas BOGO | No | Yes |

The Gold makes sense as a transit-and-basics card. Step up to the Platinum
only if you regularly use Metro Gold Class or watch movies at VOX — the
fee gap doesn't otherwise justify the upgrade.

## Best for

Early-career UAE residents on AED 5,000–12,000 a month who commute by Metro
and want a single card that handles transit, life insurance, and basic
spending. The Nol integration alone removes the need to carry a separate
top-up card.

## Watch out for

- **Earn rate is genuinely thin.** 1% on most spend is below the UAE
  cashback floor; this is a convenience card, not a rewards card
- **Plus Points realise poorly.** Conversion to Skywards Miles uses
  unfavourable internal rates; cashback at par-value is usually the better
  redemption path
- **3.49% monthly interest** is at the upper end of the UAE range — revolve
  a balance for a month and your annual earn evaporates
- **No spend-based waiver.** The AED 104 fee is fixed; budget for it
- **VAT-inclusive pricing.** The published fee is AED 103.95 incl. VAT —
  treat AED 104 as the round figure for budgeting
