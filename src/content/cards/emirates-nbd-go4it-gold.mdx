The Go4it Gold is Emirates NBD's entry-level lifestyle card, combining Nol card functionality
with a low AED 104 annual fee. Ideal for UAE residents who want RTA convenience and basic rewards
without a high income threshold.

## Earn rates

| Spend type | Plus Points per AED 1 |
|---|---|
| Weekends | **0.0125** (5 PP per AED 400) |
| RTA (transit, parking) | **0.01** (4 PP per AED 400) |
| Standard (weekday retail) | **0.01** (1 PP per AED 100) |

## Nol card integration

The card chip works as a Nol card for Dubai Metro, buses, RTA parking meters, and water buses.
Cardholder and one guest receive a complimentary Dubai Ferry ride per month.

## Notes

- Annual fee is AED 103.95 (inclusive of VAT).
- No VOX Cinemas BOGO or Metro Gold Class (step up to Platinum for those).
- Lowest-fee Nol-integrated card in the ENBD lineup — best for value-seekers on lower incomes.
- Life insurance benefit automatically included (AED 75,000 cover).
