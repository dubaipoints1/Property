The FAB Etihad Guest Infinite is First Abu Dhabi Bank's co-branded card with
Etihad Airways, issued on the Visa Infinite tier. It targets the AED 30,000-plus
salary band and earns Etihad Guest Miles directly into the cardholder's loyalty
account — no transfer step, no points-bank haircut.

## Earn rates

Earn is expressed as **Etihad Guest Miles per AED 10 spent**, not per AED 1 —

<HotTip>
The international multiplier is narrower than it first reads. FAB's Key Facts
Statement applies the headline international rate at **6 miles per AED 10** —
but **EU and UK spend earns at half** that, dropping to **3 miles per AED 10**.
For an expat whose foreign-currency spend skews to London, Paris or Frankfurt
that's a meaningful clip. The only route to a flat **7.5 miles per AED 10
across all spend** is the **Accelerator** opt-in at **AED 250 a month** — which
adds AED 3,000 a year to the running cost and only pays back at sustained six-
figure annual spend.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="fab-etihad-guest-infinite" />

The annual fee is **AED 2,625** including VAT, with no published first-year
waiver. The FX fee is **2.49%**, which is in normal range for a premium UAE
Visa Infinite and below the 3.5% the cashback cards in this set typically
charge. Minimum salary is **AED 30,000 a month** and salary transfer is not
required — unusual at this fee tier and a meaningful point of differentiation
for readers who want the card without committing their salary to FAB.

## Key conditions to know

<dl class="dp-conditions">
  <dt>Welcome bonus mechanics</dt>
  <dd>55,000 Etihad Guest Miles on card activation; supplementary cards earn 5,000 each. A separate limited-time offer pays up to 55,000 additional bonus miles for AED 100,000 of spend in the first 3 months (to 30 June 2026 per the FAB product page).</dd>

  <dt>Gold-tier fast-track</dt>
  <dd>One return Etihad flight within 6 months of card activation triggers Etihad Guest Gold-tier status — the working tier for most UAE residents, unlocking Etihad lounge access worldwide and one annual companion ticket in Business or higher.</dd>

  <dt>Tier Mile earn</dt>
  <dd>Up to 2.5 Etihad Tier Miles per AED 10 spent, capped at 20,000 Tier Miles per year. Card spend cannot satisfy the from-flying minimum on any Etihad Guest tier (10,000 for Silver, 20,000 for Gold, 40,000 for Platinum) — see the <a href="/airlines/etihad-guest/">Etihad Guest programme guide</a>.</dd>

  <dt>Redemption voucher</dt>
  <dd>75% Etihad Guest Miles redemption-cost discount voucher on AED 200,000 of annual spend — useful for stretching a long-haul Business redemption further if you hit the spend bar.</dd>

  <dt>Lounge access</dt>
  <dd>Unlimited complimentary access for cardholder plus 1 guest at 1,000+ airport lounges worldwide via the lounge programme attached to Visa Infinite — independent of Etihad Guest tier status.</dd>

  <dt>Eligibility</dt>
  <dd>Minimum salary AED 30,000/month; salary transfer not required; UAE residency required; salaried employment.</dd>
</dl>

## Watch out for

- **EU and UK spend earns half** the international rate per FAB's Key Facts
  Statement — the international multiplier looks narrower once your London,
  Paris and Frankfurt spend is on the line.
- **Accelerator costs AED 250 a month** for the flat 7.5 miles per AED 10 rate
  across all spend. Worth it only at sustained six-figure annual spend.
- **No first-year fee waiver.** The AED 2,625 is payable on day one.
- **Tier Mile cap of 20,000/year** — card spend can build part of a Silver-tier
  total, but you still need 10,000 from-flying miles for Silver, 20,000 for
  Gold, 40,000 for Platinum. You cannot card-spend your way to status.

## Is it worth it?

For a Abu Dhabi-based or northern-emirate UAE resident who flies Etihad two or
more times a year, earns AED 30,000-plus a month, and would otherwise be
earning Etihad Guest Miles at a lower rate on a Skywards-leaning portfolio:
yes, the FAB Etihad Guest Infinite is the strongest direct Etihad Guest earn
on a UAE Visa Infinite. The Gold-tier fast-track alone justifies the AED 2,625
fee in year one for anyone who can take a single return Etihad flight within
six months. The 55,000-plus-55,000 welcome combination clears the running cost
on any reasonable Etihad Guest valuation.

For a Dubai-based reader whose flying is primarily on Emirates metal, this is
the wrong card — your earning per dirham is higher on an [ENBD or FAB Skywards
co-brand](/airlines/skywards/) and your tier path is more accessible. The
FAB Etihad Guest Infinite is a specialist product for an audience whose
flag-carrier loyalty already runs through Abu Dhabi.

## Bottom line

The strongest UAE-issued direct Etihad Guest earner, with a Gold-tier
fast-track perk that pays the AED 2,625 fee back in cabin access and companion
tickets for committed Etihad flyers. **Apply** if Etihad is your primary
carrier, your salary clears AED 30,000 a month, and you'll take one return
Etihad segment in the first 6 months. **Skip** if you live in Dubai, fly
Emirates more than Etihad, or want a card that earns evenly on UAE in-store
spend — this one is built for the Abu Dhabi flyer, not the Dubai shopper.
