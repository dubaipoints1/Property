The FAB Etihad Guest Infinite is First Abu Dhabi Bank's co-branded card with
Etihad Airways, issued on the Visa Infinite tier. It targets the AED 30,000-plus
salary band and earns Etihad Guest Miles directly into the cardholder's loyalty
account — no transfer step, no points-bank haircut.

<HotTip>
The international multiplier is narrower than it first reads. FAB's Key Facts
Statement applies the headline international rate at **6 miles per AED 10** —
but **EU and UK spend earns at half** that, dropping to **3 miles per AED 10**.
For an expat whose foreign-currency spend skews to London, Paris or Frankfurt
that's a meaningful clip. The only route to a flat **7.5 miles per AED 10
across all spend** is the **Accelerator** opt-in at **AED 250 a month** — which
adds AED 3,000 a year to the running cost and only pays back at sustained six-
figure annual spend.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="fab-etihad-guest-infinite" />

The annual fee is **AED 2,625** including VAT, with no published first-year
waiver. The FX fee is **2.49%**, which is in normal range for a premium UAE
Visa Infinite and below the 3.5% the cashback cards in this set typically
charge. Minimum salary is **AED 30,000 a month** and salary transfer is not
required — unusual at this fee tier and a meaningful point of differentiation
for readers who want the card without committing their salary to FAB.

## What you get — per-benefit value

Per the [2 fils-per-mile baseline](/airlines/etihad-guest/) we use for
every UAE Etihad Guest co-brand review — the same framework used for
Skywards, applied symmetrically across the two UAE flag carriers. Add
the numbers up against the AED 2,625 annual fee.

### Welcome bonus

55,000 Etihad Guest Miles on card activation, plus a limited-time
55,000-mile bonus on AED 100,000 spend in three months (to 30 June 2026)
— 110,000 Miles total if both are triggered.
**Value to me: AED 2,200** at the 2-fil baseline on the full 110,000;
**AED 1,100** on the activation half alone. Burnt on a long-haul Business
saver one-way (AUH → JFK from ~88,000 Miles per the Etihad Guest
sweet-spot list), the realised cash-fare value typically lands AED
8,000–14,000 against the same miles — but the cost-basis figure is the
honest welcome-bonus framing for break-even math before applying.

### Gold-tier fast-track

One return Etihad flight within 6 months of activation triggers Etihad
Guest Gold-tier status. Gold unlocks Etihad lounge access worldwide,
priority check-in and boarding, extra baggage allowance, and one annual
companion ticket in Business or higher. The companion ticket is the
load-bearing benefit. Against a long-haul Business saver redemption that
typically clears AED 8,000–12,000 of cash-fare value, a companion ticket
priced at AED 1,500–2,500 of taxes and fees:
**Value to me: AED 5,500–9,500/year** for the flyer who actually uses the
companion ticket; **AED 1,200–2,000/year** on the lounge access + extra
bag for a four-trip flyer who doesn't use it. Honest line: the companion
ticket is the reason this card justifies the AED 2,625 fee.

### Visa Infinite lounge access

Unlimited cardholder + 1 guest at 1,000+ lounges worldwide. Independent
of Etihad Guest tier — useful on a non-Etihad sector where Gold lounge
access wouldn't apply. At an AED 200 walk-in equivalent per visit:
**Value to me: AED 2,400/year** for a regular flyer (six trips with a
guest); **AED 800/year** for a two-trip case. Largely duplicative with
Gold lounge access on Etihad sectors — count the marginal value on
non-Etihad legs only.

### 75% redemption-cost voucher on AED 200,000 spend

A 75% discount voucher applied to a redemption-cost basis. Against a
long-haul Business saver redemption (88,000–120,000 Miles one-way), the
voucher saves 66,000–90,000 Miles — at the 2-fil baseline,
**Value to me: AED 1,320–1,800/year** if you clear AED 200,000 of spend
and actually redeem for a Business sector. Worth zero if you don't clear
the threshold (AED 16,700/month of card spend) or don't redeem. The
highest-rate voucher among the UAE Etihad co-brands but also the highest
spend bar.

### Earn on AED 100,000 of card spend

At 7 Etihad Guest Miles per AED 10 on Etihad partner spend (~0.7 Miles
per AED 1) and 3.5 per AED 10 on general spend (~0.35 per AED 1), a
typical AED 100,000 mixed spend year (assume AED 25,000 partner, AED
75,000 general) returns ~43,750 Miles. At the 2-fil baseline:
**Value to me: AED 875/year**. EU and UK spend earns at half the
international rate, so a London-heavy traveller should mark this number
down by AED 100–200.

### Year-one Etihad Guest tier status

Year-one Gold via the fast-track is captured in the Gold-tier line above
— do not double-count. From year two, Gold lapses unless re-qualified on
flown tier miles (20,000 from flying, which card spend cannot satisfy).
**Value to me: AED 0/year** from year two on the status line specifically
— the working assumption for sustained value is that the cardholder is
either flying enough to re-qualify or accepting the drop back to Silver
or Bronze.

For an AED 30,000-salary Etihad-loyal flyer who takes one return Etihad
segment in the first six months to trigger Gold, clears AED 200,000 of
spend to unlock the voucher, uses the companion ticket on a long-haul
Business redemption, and lounges on four trips a year, the total package
delivers roughly **AED 10,500–14,500/year** of value in year one (welcome
+ companion + lounges + voucher + earn) and **AED 3,500–5,500/year** in
steady state against AED 2,625 of fee. The year-one case is strong; the
year-two case rests on the companion ticket renewing and the voucher
threshold being cleared again — neither is automatic.

## Key conditions to know

  <dt>Gold-tier fast-track</dt>

  <dd>Unlimited complimentary access for cardholder plus 1 guest at 1,000+ airport lounges worldwide via the lounge programme attached to Visa Infinite — independent of Etihad Guest tier status.</dd>

## Watch out for

- **EU and UK spend earns half** the international rate per FAB's Key Facts
  Statement — the international multiplier looks narrower once your London,
  Paris and Frankfurt spend is on the line.
- **Accelerator costs AED 250 a month** for the flat 7.5 miles per AED 10 rate
  across all spend. Worth it only at sustained six-figure annual spend.
- **No first-year fee waiver.** The AED 2,625 is payable on day one.
- **Tier Mile cap of 20,000/year** — card spend can build part of a Silver-tier
  total, but you still need 10,000 from-flying miles for Silver, 20,000 for
  Gold, 40,000 for Platinum. You cannot card-spend your way to status.

## Bottom line

For an Abu Dhabi-based or northern-emirate UAE resident who flies Etihad two
or more times a year and earns AED 30,000+ a month, this is the strongest
direct Etihad Guest earn on a UAE Visa Infinite. The Gold-tier fast-track
alone justifies the AED 2,625 fee in year one for anyone who can take a
single return Etihad flight within six months; the 55,000 + 55,000 welcome
clears the running cost on any reasonable Etihad Guest valuation.

**Apply** if Etihad is your primary carrier, salary clears AED 30,000, and
you'll take one return Etihad segment in the first 6 months. **Skip** if
you live in Dubai and fly Emirates more often — your earn per dirham is
higher on an [ENBD or FAB Skywards co-brand](/airlines/skywards/). This is
a specialist product for the Abu Dhabi flyer, not the Dubai shopper.
