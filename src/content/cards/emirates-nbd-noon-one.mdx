<HotTip>
noon Credits are not AED — they are a captive currency. A 20% return on a noon Food order
is genuine 20% off your next noon Food order; it is not cash, statement credit, miles or
points transferable elsewhere. The economics work if you would shop on noon anyway. They do
not work as a general cashback card — the off-platform rate is just 1%.
</HotTip>

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-noon-one" />

The card is free for life — AED 0 annual fee, no spend threshold, no clawback. The cost to
watch is a **1.99% foreign-currency fee**: most noon purchases settle in AED, but anything
through a non-AED merchant — or a noon site billed in another GCC currency — picks the fee
up. All published fees include 5% VAT.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

<WelcomeBonusCallout card="emirates-nbd-noon-one" />

The welcome stack runs to three pieces: **AED 500 in noon Credits** for **AED 5,000** of
spend in the first **60 days**, plus a **AED 250 YOUGotaGift HappyYOU Card** on issue, plus
a **1-year free noon One membership** (50% off thereafter). The AED 500 + AED 250 = AED
750 on AED 5,000 of qualifying spend is roughly a 15% return on the opening window — strong
by UAE no-fee-card standards. Critically, the welcome is **new-to-bank or new-to-card
only**: existing Emirates NBD cardholders are excluded and receive nothing for opening it.

## Watch out for

- **Captive-currency payout.** noon Credits redeem only on noon platforms. The 20% on noon
  Food is genuine, but only if you order noon Food. Stop shopping on noon and the balance
  effectively traps the rewards.
- **New-to-bank gate on the welcome.** Existing Emirates NBD cardholders get the earn rates
  but none of the AED 500 + AED 250 + noon One welcome. If you already hold an ENBD card,
  the maths is much weaker.
- **1% off-platform.** Outside the noon ecosystem the card is a 1% cashback — well below
  the UAE average. Carry a flat-rate cashback card for everything that isn't noon.
- **FX fee at 1.99%.** Modest by UAE standards but not zero. The no-annual-fee positioning
  shouldn't be confused with a travel card.

## Bottom line

A clean welcome-bonus product for new-to-bank residents who already live on noon. Spend
AED 2,000+ a month on noon platforms, clear AED 5,000 in the first 60 days, and the AED 500
+ AED 250 + free noon One stack is roughly 15% back on the opening window with strong
ongoing earn on spend you'd be doing anyway. **Apply** if you fit the new-to-bank criteria
and shop on noon weekly. **Skip** if you already bank with Emirates NBD (the welcome
disappears), or if your spend isn't concentrated on noon platforms — the 1% off-platform
rate makes a flat-rate AED-denominated cashback card the cleaner hold.
