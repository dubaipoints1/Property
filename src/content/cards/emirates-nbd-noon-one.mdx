The Emirates NBD Noon One Visa is co-branded with noon, the UAE-based
e-commerce group. It earns noon Loyalty rewards directly into the
cardholder's noon balance, redeemable at checkout across noon, noon Food,
noon Daily and Sivvi.

## What you actually get

Earn is up to 20% back as noon credits on noon platforms (noon Food 20%,
NowNow 10%, noon / Namshi / supermall / Minutes ~5%), capped at
AED 2,000/month across noon platforms, and 1% on all other spends. Welcome
bonus is AED 500 credit on AED 5,000 spend in the first two months for
new-to-bank or new-to-card customers — a ~10% return on the qualifying
spend. Annual fee is zero. FX fee is 0%. Salary transfer is not required;
minimum monthly salary AED 5,000.

## Who should open it

The card matches a household that shops on noon regularly — at least
AED 2,000–3,000 a month across the noon ecosystem — and is willing to
route a chunk of discretionary spend through it for two months to clear
the welcome hurdle. New-to-bank-only welcome eligibility means it works
particularly well as a first ENBD card for residents inside their first
two years in the UAE.

## Where it falls short

The 1% off-platform earn rate is unremarkable once the welcome bonus has been
claimed. noon credit is a closed-loop currency — useful only if you keep shopping
on noon. And the welcome-bonus exclusion of existing ENBD customers
means a second-card application from the bank's own customers gets nothing.

Worth opening for the AED 500 welcome credit if you fit the new-to-bank
criteria; not a long-term keeper.
