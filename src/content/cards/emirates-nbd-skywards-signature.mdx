The Skywards Signature is Emirates NBD's mid-tier co-branded card with Emirates Skywards. A solid
entry point to Emirates miles earning for UAE residents who fly Emirates but don't want to commit
to the premium Infinite fee.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are in **Skywards Miles per USD 1 equivalent** of eligible spend:

| Category | Miles per USD 1 |
|---|---|
| Emirates, flydubai | **1.5** |
| International spend (excl. EU/UK) | **1.0** |
| Domestic UAE spend | **0.75** |
| EU & UK spend | **0.5** |

Note: EU/UK earn rates are reduced per network/Skywards policy. Miles capped at the lower of your
credit limit or AED 50,000 per statement cycle.

## Welcome bonus breakdown

- **15,000 Miles** — on cumulative spend of USD 10,000 within first 3 billing statements
- **10,000 Miles** — on USD 3,750 at emirates.com or an Emirates sales office in the UAE within first 12 statements

## Signature vs Infinite

| Feature | Signature | Infinite |
|---|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 735 | AED 1,575 |
| Dining & travel earn | ~0.75–1.5 miles/USD | 2.5 miles/AED 1 |
| Min salary | AED 12,000 | AED 30,000 |
| Lounge access | Limited DragonPass | Unlimited DXB + AUH |
| Status match | — | Silver tier match (year 1) |

## Notes

- Earn rates are expressed per USD equivalent — check ENBD's current rate confirmation for exact AED conversion.
- Salary transfer not required but may improve approval odds.
