The Skywards Signature is Emirates NBD's mid-tier co-branded Visa — a direct-earn
Skywards card built for AED 12k–25k earners who fly Emirates a few times a year.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are credited as **Skywards Miles per USD 1** equivalent of eligible spend
(USD 1 ≈ AED 3.67; 1.5 miles per USD on partner categories works out to roughly
0.41 miles per AED 1):

| Category | Miles per USD 1 |
|---|---|
| Emirates, flydubai, duty free, food delivery, ride-hailing apps | **1.5** |
| International spend (non-AED, excl. EU/UK) | **1** |
| Domestic UAE spend | **0.75** |
| EU and UK spend | **0.37** |

Miles post directly to your Emirates Skywards account — no transfer step.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (year one) | AED 1,573.95 |
| Annual fee, year two onwards | AED 735 |
| Interest rate | 3.25% per month |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 12,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not strictly required (improves approval odds) |

## Welcome bonus breakdown

Up to **40,000 bonus Skywards Miles** credited on activation and qualifying spend —
headline value sits around AED 800 at our [2 fils-per-mile baseline](/airlines/skywards/),
enough to cover the year-two fee with room to spare.

## What you get — per-benefit value

Per the [2 fils-per-mile baseline](/airlines/skywards/) we use for every
UAE Skywards co-brand review. Add the numbers up against the AED 735
year-two fee.

### Welcome bonus

40,000 Skywards Miles on activation and qualifying spend.
**Value to me: AED 800** at the 2-fil baseline — clears the AED 735
year-two fee outright. A single regional Y-to-J upgrade (DXB → MAA from
30,000 miles each way at saver) typically extracts AED 1,200–1,600 of
cash-fare value against the same 40,000 miles, lifting the realised
return above the cost-basis figure (methodology on the
[Skywards programme overview](/airlines/skywards/)).

### Visa Airport Companion lounge access

Unlimited cardholder visits at 1,000+ lounges worldwide — **cardholder
only**, no guest included (the Infinite adds one). At an AED 200 walk-in
equivalent per visit: **Value to me: AED 800/year** for a four-trip-a-year
flyer travelling solo, **AED 400/year** for the two-trip case. Family
travel erodes this benefit — the guest you would have brought into a
Priority Pass lounge pays cash.

### Earn on AED 60,000 of card spend

The Signature reader profile is the AED 12k–25k earner running
AED 60,000 a year through the card. At ~0.41 Miles per AED on partner
spend (Emirates, flydubai, duty free, food delivery) and ~0.27 per AED
on domestic, blended at roughly 0.32 Miles per AED across realistic spend:
60,000 × 0.32 = ~19,000 Miles a year. At the 2-fil baseline:
**Value to me: AED 380/year**. The Signature's earn engine is
unremarkable — the case for the card rests on the welcome and lounge
access, not steady accrual.

### Golf and cinema benefits

Complimentary access to selected UAE golf courses (typically a green
fee of AED 400–600 saved per round) and BOGO cinema tickets via Visa
Entertainer. **Value to me: AED 0–600/year** — fully load-bearing on
whether you actually book a round of golf. Honest editorial line: most
cardholders do not; treat as bonus, not justification.

For an AED 12k–25k Skywards Signature holder running AED 60,000 a year
through the card, flying Emirates twice with the welcome bonus burnt on
a regional Y-to-J upgrade and using the lounge solo on each trip, the
total package delivers roughly **AED 1,600–2,200/year** of value in
year one (welcome included) and **AED 800–1,200/year** in year two
against AED 735 of fee. Year two is a positive but tight call — the
fee never waives, so plan for it.

## Watch out for

- **Earn-rate unit confusion.** The bank quotes miles per USD, not per AED.
  Mid-tier mileage cards quoted in AED typically read 2–2.5 miles per AED —
  this card's 1.5 miles per USD is roughly 0.41 miles per AED, materially less
  than that headline suggests at first read
- **No published spend-waiver.** Unlike the Infinite's AED 100,000 threshold,
  the Signature carries the AED 735 fee regardless of spend
- **Joining-fee hit.** AED 1,573.95 in year one is recovered only if you trigger
  the welcome bonus and use the miles

## Bottom line

The Signature breaks even most clearly for the AED 12k–25k earner who flies
Emirates once or twice a year and wants direct Skywards earning without the
Infinite's salary or fee bar. The AED 1,574 joining fee + AED 735 year-two
renewal is recovered when the 40,000-mile welcome bonus funds a single Y-to-J
upgrade on a regional sector — roughly AED 800 in value at our
[2 fils-per-mile baseline](/airlines/skywards/).

Above AED 100,000 of annual card spend, the Infinite earns enough more on the
same partner categories to cover its higher fee inside year one. Below the
AED 30,000 monthly salary bar the Infinite imposes, the Signature is the only
Skywards on-ramp on a UAE-issued Visa. The working rule: **apply** below
AED 30,000/month salary if you fly Emirates a few times a year; **move up to
the Infinite** once annual card spend clears AED 100,000.
