The Skywards Signature is Emirates NBD's mid-tier co-branded card with Emirates Skywards.
A solid entry point to Emirates miles earning for UAE residents who fly Emirates but
don't want to commit to the premium Infinite fee.

## Earn rates

Earn rates are credited as **Skywards Miles per USD 1** equivalent of eligible spend:

| Category | Miles per USD 1 |
|---|---|
| Emirates, flydubai, duty free, food delivery, ride-hailing apps | **1.5** |
| International spend (non-AED, excl. EU/UK) | **1** |
| Domestic UAE spend | **0.75** |
| EU and UK spend | **0.37** |

The bank denominates earn in USD across the Skywards co-brand range. At USD 1 ≈ AED 3.67,
1.5 miles per USD on partner categories is ~0.41 miles per AED 1 (or 4.1 miles per AED 10) —
useful for back-of-envelope, but the bank's earn statements always use the USD figure.

Miles post directly to your Emirates Skywards account — no monthly conversion step,
no transfer ratio loss.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Joining fee (year one) | AED 1,573.95 |
| Annual fee, year two onwards | AED 735 |
| Interest rate | 3.25% per month |
| FX fee | 1.99% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 12,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not strictly required (improves approval odds) |

## Welcome bonus breakdown

- **Up to 40,000 bonus Skywards Miles** — credited on activation and qualifying spend
- Headline value: ~AED 800 at our 2 fils-per-mile baseline (see our
  [Skywards valuation](/airlines/skywards/))

The bonus alone covers the year-two fee with room to spare, provided you
upgrade rather than redeem at the Skywards economy chart.

## Skywards Signature vs Skywards Infinite

| Dimension | Signature | [Infinite](/cards/emirates-nbd-skywards-infinite/) |
|---|---|---|
| Annual fee (year 2+) | AED 735 | AED 1,575 |
| Joining fee (year 1) | AED 1,573.95 | AED 3,148.95 |
| Minimum salary | AED 12,000 | AED 30,000 |
| Top earn rate | 1.5 miles / USD (partner) | 2 miles / USD (partner) |
| Lounge access | Visa Airport Companion (cardholder only) | Visa Airport Companion (1,000+ lounges, + 1 guest) |
| Welcome bonus | Up to 40,000 miles | Up to 100,000 miles + 1-year Rotana Rewards |

## Best for

Mid-market UAE residents who fly Emirates one or two long-haul sectors a year and
want Skywards earning without the AED 30,000/month salary gate. The card breaks
even most clearly when the welcome bonus funds a single Y-to-J upgrade on a
regional sector — that one redemption alone typically clears the two-year
fee burden.

## Watch out for

- **Earn-rate unit confusion.** The bank quotes miles per USD, not per AED.
  Mid-tier mileage cards quoted in AED typically read 2–2.5 miles per AED —
  this card's 1.5 miles per USD is roughly 0.41 miles per AED, materially less
  than that headline suggests at first read
- **No published spend-waiver.** Unlike the Infinite's AED 100,000 threshold,
  the Signature carries the AED 735 fee regardless of spend
- **Joining-fee hit.** AED 1,573.95 in year one is recovered only if you trigger
  the welcome bonus and use the miles

## Notes

- Salary transfer not required but may improve approval odds at AED 12,000/month
- Lounge access is cardholder-only (the Infinite adds one guest)
- EU/UK spend earns at half the international rate per Skywards network policy
