## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="adcb-lulu-titanium-gold" />

The card carries no annual fee at any spend level. The cost to watch is the **2.99%
non-AED foreign-currency markup** — market-standard for UAE-issued credit cards. All
published fees include 5% VAT.

## What you get — per-benefit value

LuLu Points have a published face value — 5,000 points redeem for AED 50,
i.e. 1 fil per point — so the earn prices cleanly. Counted against the
AED 0 annual fee.

### LuLu Points earn

1 point per AED 1 on general spend including LuLu, 2 on fuel — 1 and 2 fils
of till credit per dirham respectively.
**Value to me: AED 120/year** on AED 1,000 a month of LuLu spend, plus
**AED 120/year** on AED 500 a month of fuel. Small numbers, honestly small:
the 8x LuLu rate lives on the [Platinum sibling](/cards/adcb-lulu-platinum/).

### Talabat Mastercard discount

20% off one grocery or food order a month, capped at AED 14 per the
published terms.
**Value to me: AED 168/year** at full monthly utilisation; AED 0 if you
don't order on Talabat.

### Lounge access

ADCB publishes no visit count for this tier.
**Value to me: AED 200 per visit actually used** — the walk-in equivalent
we apply across reviews. Even two visits a year (AED 400) outweighs the
earn lines above, which is unusual on a no-fee entry card.

**Total package:** roughly **AED 400–800/year** for a holder who uses a
couple of lounge visits and the monthly Talabat discount, against no fee.
Modest — but everything here is upside on an AED 0 card. Above the
AED 8,000 salary bar the Platinum's 8x LuLu earn changes the maths
entirely.

## ADCB LuLu Titanium Gold vs LuLu (Platinum)

The Titanium Gold is the entry rung; the LuLu (Platinum) sibling is the higher-earn card.
If you clear the AED 8,000 salary threshold, the Platinum is the clear pick:

| Feature | Titanium Gold | [ADCB LuLu](/cards/adcb-lulu-platinum/) |
|---|---|---|
| Min salary | AED 5,000 | AED 8,000 |
| LuLu earn rate | 1 LuLu Point per AED 1 | **8 LuLu Points per AED 1** |
| Fuel earn rate | 2 LuLu Points per AED 1 | 2 LuLu Points per AED 1 |
| Other spend earn rate | 1 LuLu Point per AED 1 | 1 LuLu Point per AED 1 |
| Lounge access | Yes | Yes |
| Annual fee | Free for life | Free for life |

## Watch out for

- **The Platinum sibling is materially better above AED 8,000 salary.** Same AED 0 fee,
  8x LuLu earn, same lounge access. If you clear the bar, go Platinum.
- **No accelerated LuLu-store rate.** This entry tier earns the base 1 point per AED 1
  at LuLu — the 8x rate is reserved for the Platinum sibling.
- **Captive currency.** LuLu Points only redeem at LuLu stores in the UAE.
- **Modest off-LuLu earn.** 1 point per AED 1 on general spend is below the UAE flat-rate
  cashback average — pair with a broad cashback card.

## Bottom line

A free-for-life entry into ADCB's LuLu pair, sensibly accessible at AED 5,000 salary and
with lounge access bundled — features that are uncommon at the entry tier and make this a
reasonable first UAE credit card for an ADCB relationship. **Apply** if you sit between
AED 5,000 and AED 8,000 in salary and shop LuLu most weeks. **Skip** in favour of the
[Platinum sibling](/cards/adcb-lulu-platinum/) if you qualify for it — same AED 0 fee,
eight times the LuLu earn rate, same lounge access.
