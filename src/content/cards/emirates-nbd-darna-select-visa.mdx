The Darna Select Visa is the entry tier of Emirates NBD's Darna programme — points
earned can be applied against Dubai property obligations (rent, service charges,
developer payments) at participating providers. No annual fee, low salary threshold,
and zero FX fee mean it's also serviceable as a starter card if you don't use the
Darna mechanics.

## Earn rates

<EarnRateTable card="emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa" />

Points redeem against Darna programme bills — participating developers and
property managers only. Verify your landlord or building manager is in the
Darna network before applying.

## Fee summary

| Fee | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual fee | AED 0 |
| FX fee | 0% |
| Minimum monthly salary | AED 5,000 |
| Salary transfer | Not required |

The headline draw is the **0% FX fee** combined with the AED 0 annual cost —
rare on a UAE-issued Visa at this salary band. If you shop online in foreign
currency, the FX-fee saving alone can outweigh the modest earn rate on
travel and entertainment spend.

## Best for

UAE residents on AED 5,000+ a month who rent within the Darna network and want
to chip points off their housing costs. Also serviceable as a foreign-currency
shopping card given the 0% FX fee.

## Watch out for

- **Darna programme breadth.** Confirm your landlord, developer, or service
  charge issuer accepts Darna Points — the network is narrower than open
  cashback. If they don't participate, you're left with a 1% earn rate and
  no redemption path
- **No welcome bonus published** on the issuer page at our last verification
  — confirm with Emirates NBD before applying if a bonus is material to your
  decision
- **1% earn is uncompetitive** outside the Darna use-case — pair with a
  cashback card for groceries, fuel, and dining
