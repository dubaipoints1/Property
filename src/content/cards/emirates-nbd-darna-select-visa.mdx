<HotTip>
The 6.25% partner rate looks competitive on paper, but Darna Points have one redemption
universe: the Aldar / Darna partner network. They are not transferable to Skywards, Etihad
Guest, Marriott Bonvoy or any cash-equivalent. If your landlord, building manager or weekly
dining list does not sit inside that network, the effective rate on your real spend is the
0.75% non-partner line — which a no-fee flat-rate cashback card will beat outright. Run a
twelve-week audit of where your spend actually lands before applying.

## Fee summary

<FeeBlock card="emirates-nbd-darna-select-visa" />

The card is **AED 0 to hold**. The cost to watch is the **1.99% foreign-currency fee** — the
older "0% FX" line you may see in legacy summaries is not the current published figure. As
with any rewards card, clear the statement in full each month — the standard ENBD revolving
rate will erase any Darna value many times over.

## What you get — per-benefit value

Darna is one of the few UAE programmes with a published conversion — 10 Darna Points =
AED 1 — so the earn can carry honest AED figures. The caveat on every line below: the
value is only realisable inside the Darna partner network. No airline transfer, no
cash-out.

### 6.25% back at Darna partners

At AED 1,000 a month of genuine partner spend, 6.25% returns **Value to me:
AED 750/year** in Darna Points value; at AED 2,000 a month, **AED 1,500/year**. Run your
own partner number honestly — this line is the entire case for the card.

### 0.75% on everything else

AED 5,000 a month of non-partner spend returns **Value to me: AED 450/year** — against
AED 600/year from a plain 1% flat-rate cashback card on the same spend, paid in cash
rather than network-locked points. Treat the non-partner earn as a rounding term, not a
reason to hold.

### Welcome voucher + Darna Silver tier

AED 250 YOUGotaGift voucher on online application — modelled at **Value to me: AED 200,
one-time** under our standard 20% voucher discount. Darna Silver tier has no published
AED value; treat it as a discount layer, not a number.

**Total package:** a household with AED 1,000–2,000 a month of genuine partner spend
books roughly **AED 750–1,500/year** in Darna Points value plus the one-time AED 200
voucher, against no annual fee. A free card with a real return — but only if the partner
exposure is real. If it isn't, the countable value collapses to the 0.75% line and a
flat cashback card beats it outright.

## Watch out for

- **Confirm your Darna partner exposure before applying.** The 6.25% rate only pays at
  outlets inside the Aldar / Darna network. If your rent goes to a non-Aldar landlord and
  your grocery and fuel spend is at non-partner merchants, you are earning 0.75% on the
  bulk of your card spend — a no-fee flat-rate cashback card will earn more.
- **No airline or hotel transfers.** Darna Points have one home: the Aldar / Darna partner
  network. There is no points-to-Skywards or points-to-Marriott escape valve. Hold this
  for the Aldar use case, not as an open-ended rewards account.
- **The welcome offer is a voucher, not a points bonus.** AED 250 face value on
  YOUGotaGift, discounted to AED 200 on our calculator. Useful, not transformative.
- **Clawback on closure.** Standard ENBD card terms claw back welcome offers and tier
  status if the account is closed inside the first twelve months. If you are likely to
  leave the UAE or switch banks within the year, the voucher value is at risk.

## Bottom line

For the Aldar-adjacent household — tenants in Aldar communities, Yas Mall regulars,
households routing dining and weekend entertainment through the Aldar list — the Darna
Select is an easy free keep. The 6.25% partner rate is real money, the AED 250 welcome
voucher is useful, and Darna Silver adds a modest discount layer, with no annual fee or
salary-transfer commitment to balance against.

It's the wrong default card for anyone else: the 0.75% non-partner rate is well below a
flat-rate cashback card, and Darna Points have no exit ramp out of the Aldar ecosystem.
**Apply** as a secondary card if your spend list genuinely lives at Darna partners;
**skip** as a primary — pair it with a broad cashback card if you take it at all.
