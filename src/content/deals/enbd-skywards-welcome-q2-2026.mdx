Emirates NBD is offering up to **100,000 bonus Skywards Miles** to new Skywards
Infinite cardholders, plus a complimentary year of Rotana Rewards membership —
per the issuer product page, verified 11 June 2026. The current cycle runs
through 30 June 2026.

- **Card:** Skywards Infinite
- **Bonus:** Up to 100,000 Skywards Miles + 1-year Rotana Rewards membership
- **Spend requirement:** ENBD does not publish the qualifying-spend mechanics
  on the product page — confirm the threshold and window in writing at
  application
- **Eligibility:** New-to-card customers; UAE residents earning AED 30,000+ per month
- **Expires:** 30 June 2026

An earlier version of this deal note carried a 40,000-mile figure from a
previous cycle; the issuer page now headlines up to 100,000. Verify terms on
the [Emirates NBD application page](https://www.emiratesnbd.com/en/cards/credit-cards/skywards-infinite-credit-card)
before applying — promotional terms and the welcome-mile cap can change
without notice.
