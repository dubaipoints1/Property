Emirates NBD is offering up to **40,000 Skywards Miles** to new Skywards Infinite
cardholders who hit AED 25,000 of eligible spend within the first 90 days. The promotion
runs through 30 June 2026; the welcome miles credit to the cardholder's Skywards account
within 60 days of clearing the spend hurdle.

- **Card:** Skywards Infinite
- **Bonus:** 40,000 Skywards Miles
- **Spend requirement:** AED 25,000 in 90 days
- **Eligibility:** New-to-card customers only; UAE residents earning AED 30,000+ per month
- **Expires:** 30 June 2026

Verify terms on the [Emirates NBD application page](https://www.emiratesnbd.com) before
applying — promotional terms and the welcome-mile cap can change without notice.
