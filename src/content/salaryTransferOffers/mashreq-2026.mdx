## Real-world notes

- Verified 30 June 2026 against Mashreq's published **Bonus on Salary Transfer**
  Terms & Conditions (PDF) and the live NEO salary-transfer page.
- The band figures here are the **core joining bonus** from the T&C
  (AED 200 / 500 / 1,000 / 2,500). Mashreq's marketing headline "up to
  AED 3,500" bundles additional early-bird / account components not part of this
  fixed salary-band table — we list only the verifiable per-band bonus.
- Branded "cashback" by Mashreq, but it is a **fixed AED bonus per salary band**
  paid as cash to the account over 6 months — not an ongoing percentage cashback.
- No published hard end date; eligibility is account-open-date based and the
  structure continues into a "NEO Salary Bonus 2026" T&C effective 1 August 2026.
  Re-verify periodically; `validUntil` is set to year-end as a conservative marker.
- The clawback is unusually long-tailed: a 2-month salary-transfer gap inside the
  first 12 months reverses **all** instalments already paid.
