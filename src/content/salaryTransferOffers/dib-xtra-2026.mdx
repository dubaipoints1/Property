## Real-world notes

- Verified 30 June 2026 against DIB's published **DIB XTRA Salary Transfer
  Campaign** Terms & Conditions (PDF). Campaign start date 1 January 2026; no
  published hard end date (`validUntil` set to year-end as a conservative marker).
- The band figures here are the **salary-transfer** column of the T&C table for
  standard/resident customers (AED 250 / 500 / 1,000 / 2,500 / 3,000). **UAE
  Nationals** get a higher salary-transfer table (AED 500 / 1,000 / 2,000 /
  3,500 / 5,500).
- DIB's marketing headline "up to AED 16,000" is the **sum** of the salary-
  transfer bonus plus separate personal-finance, auto-finance, home-finance and
  covered-card bonuses — achievable only by taking the full bundle. We list only
  the verifiable salary-transfer component.
- The catch is the bundle: unlike the Mashreq bonus, DIB's salary-transfer bonus
  is **not standalone** — it pays only if you also take an eligible covered card
  or finance facility. Weigh the bonus against the cost/commitment of that product.
- This is the only **Sharia-compliant** offer currently in the tracker.
