## Real-world notes

> **Archive note (28 May 2026):** The cash-bonus structure tracked above is
> based on an earlier ENBD campaign and is no longer the live offer. ENBD's
> [current salary-transfer promotion](https://www.emiratesnbd.com/en/campaigns/transfer-your-salary-earn-more)
> (valid 1 February – 30 June 2026) is a **bonus-interest** offer — base
> 5.00% p.a. for residents (5.50% for Emiratis), plus an additional 0.50%
> if the customer takes a credit card and spends AED 10,000 in 60 days,
> paid on the average monthly account balance over a 6-month reward
> window. Minimum salary transfer AED 10,000, minimum average monthly
> balance AED 25,000.
>
> The current offer's reward shape (bonus interest on balance, not a
> fixed cash sum per salary band) does not map onto the
> `salaryTransferOfferShape` schema's `cash | voucher | cashback_monthly | points`
> reward types — noted as a schema-extension candidate
> (e.g. `rewardType: "interest_bonus"`). Until the schema is extended,
> the bank hub describes the offer in prose; this file is
> archived so the tracker does not surface the stale cash-bonus
> structure.
