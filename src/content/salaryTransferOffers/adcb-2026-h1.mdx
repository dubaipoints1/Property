> **Verification status:** illustrative skeleton. Confirm exact bands and split

- The headline "AED 12,000" requires hitting **both** the salary transfer
  tenure **and** the card spend tier — easy to miss on the marketing page.

  by a small amount.
- ADCB cards earn TouchPoints, separately convertible to Skywards / Etihad
  Guest. The card spend bonus here is paid as cash, not TouchPoints.
